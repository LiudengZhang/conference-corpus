# Xenium RNA+Protein — 10x Genomics

**10x's in-situ multiomics expansion of the Xenium platform: 500-plex RNA co-registered with 28-plex protein, commercial mid-2026.**

## At a glance

- **Vendor / launch date / availability:** 10x Genomics; announced at AGBT 2026 (Marco Island, Feb 22–25); **commercial availability mid-2026** *per [GenomeWeb — AGBT showcases diversity of spatial genomics technologies, Feb 2026](https://www.genomeweb.com/business-news/agbt-showcases-diversity-spatial-genomics-technologies)*.
- **Chemistry / modality:** In-situ multiomics — combined RNA in-situ hybridization + antibody-based protein detection on the Xenium analyzer.
- **RNA plex (PR):** **500-plex RNA** *per [GenomeWeb AGBT coverage, Feb 2026](https://www.genomeweb.com/business-news/agbt-showcases-diversity-spatial-genomics-technologies)* **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)**.
- **Protein plex (PR):** **28-plex protein** *per [GenomeWeb AGBT coverage, Feb 2026](https://www.genomeweb.com/business-news/agbt-showcases-diversity-spatial-genomics-technologies)* **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)**.
- **List price / reagent cost:** Not disclosed at AGBT — **TBD**.

## What's new vs prior generation

Xenium RNA+Protein adds an antibody-based protein channel co-registered with the existing Xenium in-situ RNA workflow. The 500-plex RNA target list is consistent with current Xenium 5K-class panels (subset selection), and the 28-plex protein channel is the first time 10x has shipped joint RNA+protein on Xenium hardware rather than requiring serial sections. Positioned to close the gap to CosMx / MERSCOPE proteomics roadmaps.
