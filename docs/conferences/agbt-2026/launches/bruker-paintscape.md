## What's new vs prior generation

PaintScape is Bruker Spatial Biology's roadmap entry into 3D genome visualization — distinct from sequencing-based Hi-C / 3C methods in that it targets in-situ spatial readout of nuclear chromatin organization. Public AGBT coverage frames it as a companion to CellScape (proteomics) in Bruker's 2026 spatial dual-track refresh, but the underlying chemistry (FISH-based chromosome painting, oligopaint, or other) is not specified in available sources — flagged as sparse.
