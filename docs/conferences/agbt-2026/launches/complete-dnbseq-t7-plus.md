# DNBSEQ-T7+ — Complete Genomics (Swiss Rockets)

**The refreshed DNB short-read flagship — 48B reads / 24h across four flow cells, $100 genome list, and a new corporate parent (Swiss Rockets) announced at AGBT 2026.**

## At a glance

- **Vendor / launch date / availability:** Complete Genomics (announced at AGBT 2026); strategic license / acquisition by Swiss Rockets disclosed week of AGBT; shipping in US/Canada now *per [Complete Genomics AGBT 2026 PR](https://www.completegenomics.com/complete-genomics-highlights-dnbseq-t7-at-agbt-2026-expanding-high-throughput-sequencing-capability/)* and *[Swiss Rockets acquisition announcement](https://swissrockets.com/news/swiss-rockets-announces-strategic-exclusive-license-acquisition-of-cutting-edge-sequencing-technology-from-mgi-tech-and-complete-genomics)*.
- **Chemistry / modality:** DNB (DNA nanoball) short-read SBS; four-flow-cell configuration.
- **Throughput (PR):** **48 billion reads / 24h** with four flow cells; ~28,000 30× human genomes per year per instrument *per [Complete Genomics AGBT 2026 PR](https://www.completegenomics.com/complete-genomics-highlights-dnbseq-t7-at-agbt-2026-expanding-high-throughput-sequencing-capability/)* **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)**.
- **Read length:** Standard paired-end (2×150 and shorter PE configurations).
- **Accuracy (PR):** Q40 per-base *per [Complete Genomics AGBT 2026 PR](https://www.completegenomics.com/complete-genomics-highlights-dnbseq-t7-at-agbt-2026-expanding-high-throughput-sequencing-capability/)* **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)**.
- **List price / cost-per-genome:** **$800k** instrument; **$100 / 30× human genome**; $1 / Gb *per [Complete Genomics AGBT 2026 PR](https://www.completegenomics.com/complete-genomics-highlights-dnbseq-t7-at-agbt-2026-expanding-high-throughput-sequencing-capability/)*.

## What's new vs prior generation

The "+" refresh is a throughput and accuracy bump on the existing DNBSEQ-T7 platform — pushing the four-flow-cell ceiling to 48B reads/24h and lifting per-base accuracy to a Q40 PR claim. The accompanying corporate change is the bigger headline: [Swiss Rockets' announced license / acquisition](https://swissrockets.com/news/swiss-rockets-announces-strategic-exclusive-license-acquisition-of-cutting-edge-sequencing-technology-from-mgi-tech-and-complete-genomics) restructures the route-to-market for MGI / Complete Genomics technology in regions where MGI has faced US import-restriction headwinds, providing the western commercial vehicle for the platform.

## Reception / competitive context

DNBSEQ-T7+ is the **$100 / genome twin** to Element VITARI ($689k list, $100 / genome) — the two are the AGBT 2026 short-read lineup's joint price floor, sitting below Ultima UG200 ($850k–$1.25M) and above Roche Axelios's $750k / $150 genome target. [GenomeWeb's "battle royale" recap](https://www.genomeweb.com/sequencing/agbt-sequencing-tech-updates-portend-battle-royale-high-throughput-clinical-applications) calls out the Swiss Rockets acquisition as the policy-relevant pivot that lets Complete Genomics compete in US clinical accounts that had been hesitant on MGI-branded hardware.
