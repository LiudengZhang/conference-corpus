# Atera — 10x Genomics

**10x's next-generation spatial platform: 18k-gene whole-transcriptome amplification + stackable 1k-gene panels; AGBT roadmap preview, formal launch deferred to AACR April 2026.**

## At a glance

- **Vendor / launch date / availability:** 10x Genomics; roadmap preview at AGBT 2026 (Feb 22–25); **formal launch at AACR April 2026**; ships H2 2026 *per [10x Genomics Atera PR, Apr 2026](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/10x-genomics-introduces-atera-a-new-platform-to-redefine-how-biology-is-measured-and-understood-302746542.html)*.
- **Chemistry / modality:** Whole-transcriptome spatial amplification + stackable targeted panels.
- **WTA plex (PR):** **18k-gene whole-transcriptome amplification** *per [10x Atera spec page, Apr–May 2026](https://www.10xgenomics.com/instruments/atera-instrument)* **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)**.
- **Panel plex (PR):** Stackable 1k-gene targeted panels on top of WTA *per [10x Atera spec page, Apr–May 2026](https://www.10xgenomics.com/instruments/atera-instrument)* **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)**.
- **List price:** **$495k instrument** *per [10x Atera PR, Apr 2026](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/10x-genomics-introduces-atera-a-new-platform-to-redefine-how-biology-is-measured-and-understood-302746542.html)*.
- **Reagent cost:** ~**$2,200 per sample WTA reagent** *per [10x Atera PR, Apr 2026](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/10x-genomics-introduces-atera-a-new-platform-to-redefine-how-biology-is-measured-and-understood-302746542.html)* **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)**.

## What's new vs prior generation

Atera is 10x's first instrument to combine an unbiased 18k-gene whole-transcriptome readout with stackable 1k-gene targeted panels in the same workflow — answering the Visium HD vs Xenium "panel-or-WTA" choice with a single platform. Positioned to defend 10x's spatial franchise against Bruker CosMx mouse-whole-transcriptome and Vizgen MERSCOPE Ultra panel expansion.

## Headline benchmarks

> 18k-gene WTA + stackable 1k-gene panels, $495k instrument, ~$2,200/sample WTA reagent **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)** — *[10x Genomics PR, Apr 2026](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/10x-genomics-introduces-atera-a-new-platform-to-redefine-how-biology-is-measured-and-understood-302746542.html)*.

Resolution (subcellular vs single-cell), detection efficiency, and head-to-head with Visium HD / Xenium not yet in public coverage — **TBD pending H2 2026 shipments**.

## Reception / competitive context

[GenomeWeb's Q1 2026 earnings coverage](https://www.genomeweb.com/business-news/10x-genomics-sees-dimmed-spatial-bio-sales-atera-rolls-out-reports-3-percent-q1) frames Atera as the centerpiece of 10x's 2026 spatial refresh in a quarter of dimmed Visium / Xenium sales. AGBT 2026 served as the roadmap preview venue; AACR April 2026 was the formal launch. Strategic positioning: vs Visium HD (panel breadth) and Xenium (single-cell resolution) within 10x's own lineup; vs Bruker CosMx and Vizgen MERSCOPE Ultra externally.
