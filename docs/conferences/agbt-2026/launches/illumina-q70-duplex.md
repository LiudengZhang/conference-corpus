# Q70 duplex kits — Illumina

## At a glance

- **Vendor:** Illumina
- **Launch date / venue:** AGBT 2026 (Marco Island, FL, Feb 22–25, 2026)
- **Availability:** Early access H2 2026; broad rollout TBD *per [Illumina AGBT 2026 conference page](https://www.illumina.com/events/conferences/2026/agbt-2026.html)*
- **Modality:** Duplex chemistry kit for NovaSeq X (instrument-compatible, kit-only)
- **Target accuracy:** **Q70** duplex *per Illumina AGBT 2026 PR* **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)**
- **List price:** TBD (kit-only; no new instrument capex)

## What's new

Q70 duplex kits are a chemistry upgrade for the installed NovaSeq X / X Plus base — no instrument swap required. The pitch is a 100× error-rate reduction over conventional Q40 short-read calls by using duplex (both strands of a molecule) consensus, putting Illumina in direct accuracy contention with PacBio HiFi and Roche Axelios SBX duplex on per-base error, while retaining NovaSeq throughput economics.

## Headline benchmarks

- **Q70 target accuracy** — vendor ceiling claim; no third-party concordance or rare-variant validation has been disclosed at AGBT *per [Illumina AGBT 2026 page](https://www.illumina.com/events/conferences/2026/agbt-2026.html)* **(vendor PR — no independent benchmark)**.
- Throughput / cost-per-Gb impact vs. standard NovaSeq X kits not yet quantified in public materials.

## Reception / competitive context

Q70 is Illumina's accuracy-ceiling answer to Roche Axelios 1 (Q38 duplex, long-read SBX) and PacBio SPRQ-Hq (HiFi at SMRT-cell-reuse economics). Because it ships on installed NovaSeq X fleets, the switching cost for existing high-throughput labs is reagent-only, which trade press flagged as the strategically important detail of Illumina's AGBT 2026 announcement bundle *per [GEN AGBT 2026 recap](https://www.genengnews.com/topics/omics/agbt-2026-recap-ngs-big-bets-and-spatials-rising-momentum/)*.
