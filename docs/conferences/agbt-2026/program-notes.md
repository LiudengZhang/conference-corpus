### GBCC 2025 / Bioconductor cross-refs

The Bioconductor spatial stack ([SpatialExperiment](https://bioconductor.org/packages/release/bioc/html/SpatialExperiment.html), [SpatialFeatureExperiment](https://bioconductor.org/packages/3.20/bioc/html/SpatialFeatureExperiment.html), Voyager, spatialLIBD, SOSTA, SpatialData) is platform-agnostic by design, but its release-cycle relevance scales with whichever spatial platform wins shelf space. Specifically:

- **Xenium / Xenium RNA+Protein** output formats — primary consumer of SpatialFeatureExperiment + SpatialExperiment integrations.
- **MERSCOPE / MERFISH 2.0** — Voyager and SpatialData both have MERSCOPE readers.
- **CosMx (Bruker)** — SpatialExperiment / SpatialData both support CosMx ingestion.
- **Atera (10x)** — readers will need to be written once Atera ships H2 2026.
