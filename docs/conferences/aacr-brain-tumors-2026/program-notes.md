## Format & embargo

AACR special-conference sessions historically use:

- **Plenary keynotes** — typically 3–5 named keynote talks bookending the program; Mario Suva and Simona Parrinello (UCL, "Role of injury programs in gliomagenesis") confirmed for 2026.
- **Concurrent thematic sessions** — clustered around major tracks (cellular therapy, IDH-mut, neuroscience of gliomas, pediatric, brain mets, single-cell / spatial, BBB delivery, diagnostics / biomarkers).
- **Poster sessions** — one or two poster sessions running across days 2 and 3; abstracts on the AACR Brain 26 abstracts page.
- **Educational / forum sessions** — typically a single shorter educational block for early-career investigators.
- **Industry sessions / sponsored research presentations** — Servier (vorasidenib), Day One (tovorafenib), Chimerix (ONC201), Novocure (TTF), cellular-therapy sponsors.
- **Trainee / early-career awards** — AACR-sponsored travel awards and trainee posters; results visible in *Cancer Research* supplement.

**Embargo policy:** AACR special-conference abstracts publish to the *Cancer Research* online supplement on or around the day of presentation. Sponsor topline press releases for the larger clinical programs (e.g., Servier, Day One, Chimerix, CARGO, 2seventy) frequently precede embargo. Detailed 2026 embargo timing was implemented per AACR press-program standard; trade-press recaps in OncLive / Targeted Oncology / Neurology Live / CancerNetwork landed Mar 23 – Apr 4.

## Top 5 anticipated themes — pre-meeting watch list (reconciled retrospectively)

Reconstructed from the public AACR Brain 26 program page, chair public statements (Bagley / Monje / Suva), Penn Medicine institutional roundup, and the cross-cutting threads in the field as of Q1 2026. **Each entry is followed by a short reconciliation note for the post-meeting update.**

1. **GBM CAR-T architecture convergence — single-target vs bivalent vs bicistronic.** UPenn ICV bivalent (EGFR + IL-13Rα2) Ph1 update (Bagley / O'Rourke / June lab; published in Nature Medicine 2024 / PMC 2025) vs City of Hope locoregional IL-13Rα2 single-target Ph1 (Brown / Badie / Forman lab; Nature Medicine 2024) vs chlorotoxin-targeted (Brown / Badie) vs EGFRvIII single-target lessons-learned. The 2026 question: which architecture / route / target combination beats single-target antigen escape, and where do "armoured" constructs (TGF-β-resistant, IL-7 / IL-15) and logic-gated designs (SynNotch dual-signal) fit. *Reconciliation: track which Ph1 cohorts had cohort-expansion announcements during the meeting week.*
2. **GD2-CAR-T in pediatric DIPG / DMG — the inflection signal.** Monje + Mackall + Stanford / partner-site Ph1 in H3K27M-mutant DMG showed 9/10 evaluable clinical benefit in earlier reads; the 2026 update is the expanded cohort + biomarker / mechanism data. Practice-defining for pediatric DMG (a disease that has had essentially zero practice-changing readouts since ONC201 / dordaviprone entered the picture). *Reconciliation: tie to ONC201 / dordaviprone H3K27M Ph2 + Ph3 trajectory and to Chimerix FDA filing window.*
3. **Vorasidenib (Voranigo) post-INDIGO — combinations and high-grade extension.** First-in-class brain-penetrant mutant IDH1/2 inhibitor approved Aug 2024 for grade 2 IDH-mut astrocytoma / oligodendroglioma. The 2026 biology + early-clinical question: what's the combination partner (TTF per the Terasaki / Novocure CDA program; RT; immune; vaccine)? Does it extend usefully to grade 3 / 4 IDH-mut (high-grade) glioma (the CURE-reported safety + tolerability signal points this way)? *Reconciliation: track Servier post-marketing surveillance + Ph2 combination registrations.*
4. **IDH-mutant glioma neoantigen / peptide vaccine signal.** Platten / Sahm / Schumacher-aligned DKFZ IDH1R132H peptide vaccine (NOA-16 paradigm extended) is the most-watched neuro-IO + IDH-mut combination strategy. Cross-link to broader neoantigen-vaccine field at SITC 2026 (Cathy Wu Smalley Lecture). *Reconciliation: any 2026 cohort-expansion or vaccine + checkpoint data.*
5. **Neuroscience of gliomas — neural-circuit integration and microglia.** Monje-thread (neuron-glioma synaptic integration, AMPAR-driven proliferation, activity-dependent growth) + dopamine signalling (Venkatesh / Monje-aligned) + microglia / TAM (Bowman / Joyce / Quail-aligned) converging into a single neuro-immune-circuit framework. The 2026 update tests whether the framework yields druggable nodes (e.g., gabapentinoid / glutamate-modulator clinical signals; CSF-1R inhibitors; specific microglial-reprogramming approaches). *Reconciliation: any window-of-opportunity / Phase-0 trials testing circuit-modulator + standard-of-care.*

- **MSD / BMS** — checkpoint approaches in CNS (the persistent unmet niche; window-of-opportunity Ph0).
- **BioMed Valley Discoveries / Stemline / Kazia / various IDH small-molecule sponsors** — IDH-mut combination programs.

## Tracks expected to dominate the corpus

### Cellular therapy for CNS tumors

- **Adult GBM CAR-T architecture:** single-target IL-13Rα2 (City of Hope), single-target EGFRvIII (lessons-learned), bivalent / bicistronic (UPenn ICV EGFR + IL-13Rα2), chlorotoxin (City of Hope), B7-H3, multi-antigen designs.
- **Pediatric CAR-T:** GD2 (Stanford DMG; medulloblastoma), B7-H3 (Seattle Children's medulloblastoma + ependymoma + ATRT), GPC2, multi-antigen pediatric medulloblastoma pipeline.
- **Engineering / next-gen designs:** TGF-β-resistant; IL-7 / IL-15-armoured; logic-gated SynNotch; cell-state-directed antigen selection.
- **Delivery routes:** intratumoral, intraventricular (ICV), CSF, IV; locoregional vs IV + ICV trade-offs.
- **Endogenous-immune interplay:** epitope spreading; TCR-clonotype dynamics; neoantigen response in CAR-T responders.

### IDH-mutant glioma

- **Vorasidenib post-INDIGO biology** and combinations (TTF, RT, vaccine, immune).
- **2-HG biology and downstream epigenetic targeting.**
- **High-grade IDH-mut clinical experience** (safety / tolerability extending into grade 3 / 4 per CURE-reported data).
- **IDH-mut neoantigen / peptide vaccine** (DKFZ extended; combination with checkpoint).

### Glioblastoma biology

- **Cell-state plasticity** (AC-/MES-/OPC-/NPC-like, Suva / Tirosh / Bernstein framework).
- **Resistance mechanisms** (intratumoral heterogeneity, clonal evolution under therapy).
- **TAM / microglia biology** (Bowman / Joyce / Quail-aligned spatial and single-cell work).
- **Cancer-neuroscience integration** (Monje + Venkatesh, neural-circuit-glioma).

### Pediatric brain tumors

- **Medulloblastoma molecular subgroup-specific therapy** (WNT, SHH, Group 3, Group 4).
- **DIPG / DMG** (H3K27M, ONC201, GD2-CAR-T, B7-H3-CAR-T, CED-delivered approaches).
- **Pediatric LGG** (BRAF / RAF / MEK; tovorafenib post-FIREFLY).
- **Ependymoma, ATRT, embryonal tumors.**

### Brain metastases

- **HER2 brain mets** (T-DXd CNS activity, tucatinib + ADC combinations).
- **Lung / melanoma brain mets** (CNS-penetrant targeted-therapy patterns).
- **Leptomeningeal disease** (intrathecal regimens).
- **Cross-cancer brain-mets practice question** — bridges to SABCS 2026, ASCO 2026 GU / Lung / Melanoma readouts.

### Neuroscience of gliomas

- **Neural-circuit integration** (Monje thread).
- **Dopamine and other neurotransmitter signalling.**
- **Microglia and TAM biology.**
- **Astrocyte-glioma crosstalk.**

### Blood-brain-barrier delivery

- **BBB / BTB-aware drug design** (Phase-0 / window-of-opportunity, Ivy Brain Tumor Center).
- **Focused-ultrasound BBB opening** (Sunnybrook).
- **Convection-enhanced delivery** (pediatric DIPG / DMG).
- **CNS-active ADCs as a class question.**

### Single-cell / spatial CNS biology

- **scRNA / snRNA atlases** (GLASS Consortium, CBTN / OpenPBTA).
- **Spatial transcriptomics** (Visium HD / Xenium glioma applications).
- **Multiplex IF for TAM / immune** profiling.
- **3D-genome organization in glioma** (Bernstein-aligned).

### Diagnostics / biomarkers

- **DKFZ methylation classifier (MNP v13)** — pediatric brain-tumor diagnosis standard.
- **CSF ctDNA / cfDNA** for response monitoring.
- **Imaging biomarkers** for response assessment (RANO 2.0).

### Neuro-immuno-oncology beyond CAR-T

- **Checkpoint blockade in GBM** (persistent unmet niche; Phase-0 / window-of-opportunity).
- **Oncolytic virus** (CD19 + EGFRvIII delivery per Nature Comms 2026; OV-priming-CAR combinations).
- **Vaccine approaches** beyond IDH (EGFRvIII variant-peptide; personalized neoantigen).
