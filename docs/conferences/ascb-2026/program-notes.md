# Cell Bio 2026 — Program Notes

Pre-meeting working notes. The meeting is **7 months out as of May 11, 2026**. Most of this document is forward-looking and reconstructed from the ASCB-announced keynote / symposia slate, Cell Bio 2025 program shape, the published Cell Bio 2026 abstract category list, and bioRxiv signal in the AACR / EuroBioC / GBCC corpus that previews ASCB-bound work. Anything not explicitly cited to a Cell Bio 2026 announcement is **speculative**, flagged inline, and to be reconciled when the program-at-a-glance posts (~Sep – Oct 2026).

## Verified meeting facts

- **Meeting:** Cell Bio 2026 — An ASCB | EMBO Meeting (joint annual meeting of the American Society for Cell Biology and the European Molecular Biology Organization)

  - **Liqun Luo, PhD** (Stanford / HHMI) — neural-circuit assembly, single-neuron tracing, cell-type taxonomy
  - **Elly Tanaka, PhD** (IMBA, Austrian Academy of Sciences) — regeneration biology, blastema cell-state programming
- **Symposia:** 16 named additional speakers from Yale, Fred Hutch, King's College London, UC San Diego, Washington University, UCLA, ETH Zurich, and others (full slate on Cell Bio 2026 site)
- **Abstract submission window:**
  - **Apr 7, 2026:** submission site opens
  - **Jun 9, 2026:** talk and early-poster deadline (member $95 / nonmember $125)
  - **Aug 4, 2026:** late-poster deadline (member $165 / nonmember $195)

## Expected tracks (per ASCB 2026 abstract category list — verified)

ASCB has published the full abstract category list for Cell Bio 2026 (50+ topic areas across major categories). Major categories that anchor the program:

1. **Genome biology and RNA biology** — DNA replication / recombination, chromatin structure, transcription, RNA processing / non-coding RNA
2. **Membrane biology and trafficking** — ER, Golgi, endosomal / lysosomal, secretion, exocytosis, membrane contact sites
3. **Cytoskeleton and motility** — actin, microtubules, intermediate filaments, cell migration, mechanics
4. **Signal transduction** — kinase cascades, GPCR, second messengers, mechanotransduction
5. **Organelles and cell division** — mitosis, meiosis, centrosomes, cytokinesis, organelle inheritance
6. **Cell metabolism and stress response** — autophagy, UPR / ER stress, integrated stress response, mitochondrial biology
7. **Cell death and aging** — apoptosis, necroptosis, ferroptosis, senescence, mitophagy
8. **Disease mechanisms** — neurodegeneration, infection / host – pathogen, inflammation
9. **Developmental and stem cell biology** — pluripotency, lineage commitment, organoids / assembloids
10. **Cancer cell biology** — *the explicit AACR overlap category*
11. **Immune cell biology** — innate / adaptive at the cellular level (modest SITC overlap)
12. **Plant and evolutionary cell biology** — comparative / non-model cell biology
13. **Advanced tools and technologies** — microscopy, image analysis, genome engineering, reporter systems
14. **Systems biology and bioengineering** — modeling, synthetic biology, organ-on-chip, microfluidics
15. **Science education** — pedagogy / DEI track

## Anticipated themes — top 5 to watch

**All entries below are speculative**, sourced from the Cell Bio 2025 program, the keynote slate composition for 2026, AACR 2026 single-cell / spatial echo, and bioRxiv preprint pacing in the relevant areas.

### Theme 1 — Organelle contact sites as the new functional unit

ER – mitochondria, ER – lysosome, ER – plasma-membrane, mitochondria – lysosome contact-site biology has matured from descriptive cataloguing (2015 – 2020) into mechanism (2021 – 2024) and now into therapeutic substrate (2025 – 2026). The Hegde keynote on ER membrane biology and protein quality control sits squarely in this lineage. Expect symposia / minisymposia where:

- Contact-site tethers (VAPA / B, ORPs, PDZD8) are characterized in cancer / neurodegeneration contexts
- Lipid transfer at contact sites is linked to autophagy initiation and ferroptosis sensitivity
- AACR overlap: ferroptosis-induction therapeutic strategies, autophagy modulator clinical programs

### Theme 2 — Autophagy / lysosomal biology as therapeutic mechanism

A continuing thread from the Cell Bio 2024 and 2025 programs. ULK1 / ATG complex regulation, selective autophagy receptors (NBR1, p62, NDP52, OPTN), mitophagy, ferritinophagy, lysosomal exocytosis. Therapeutic relevance accelerates from Deciphera, Sutro, autophagy-modulator clinical programs and the broader proteostasis / TPD axis. Expect:

- Selective autophagy of organelles (mitophagy, ER-phagy, lipophagy) in cancer cell biology
- Lysosomal biogenesis / TFEB axis as druggable
- AACR overlap: KEAP1 / NRF2 axis, NSCLC autophagy-dependence sub-segments, pancreatic autophagy programs

### Theme 3 — AI / foundation models for cell biology and bioimaging

New surface. Cell-state foundation models, virtual-cell predictions, and image-foundation models are starting to appear in Cell Bio programs. The Luo keynote on cell-type taxonomy (single-neuron tracing) is methodologically adjacent to this thread. Expect:

- Image-foundation models (CellSAM, MorphoNet) demonstrated on cell-biology workflows
- Cellpose 3 / Stardist updates in tool talks
- Virtual-cell models (CZI Virtual Cell Initiative, Tahoe-100M class) in cell-state prediction
- AACR overlap: virtual-cells axis at AACR 2026 (ED03 educational session and topics/virtual-cells dossier)
- MICCAI overlap: medical-imaging foundation-model work crosses into cell-bio imaging

### Theme 4 — Super-resolution + expansion microscopy + lattice light-sheet convergence

Imaging hardware and computational pipeline updates that are characteristic of ASCB's Advanced Tools track:

- MINFLUX / DNA-PAINT / SMLM continued evolution and accessibility
- Expansion microscopy with iterative protocols (e.g., 10x ExM, X10 ExM, magnify) for ultrastructural cell biology at light-microscope cost
- Lattice light-sheet + adaptive optics in live tissue
- Open-source image analysis (Cellpose 3, Stardist, napari plugins) and ZeroCostDL4Mic-style training resources
- Cross-link: EuroBioC 2025 HistoImagePlot, GBCC 2025 image-analysis workshops, MICCAI 2026 medical imaging methods

### Theme 5 — Organoid / assembloid cell-state programming + regeneration biology

The Tanaka keynote (axolotl regeneration, blastema cell-state) frames the regeneration thread. Convergence with stem-cell organoid programs:

- Brain / cortical assembloids and neural-circuit reconstruction at organoid scale
- Gut, lung, liver, kidney organoid maturation and cell-state taxonomies
- Single-cell + spatial mapping of organoid heterogeneity
- AACR overlap: cancer organoid models in pre-clinical drug-discovery sessions
- Cross-link: AACR 2026 single-cell axis, ASCO 2026 patient-derived-organoid trial cohorts

## Anticipated tool / method talks to watch

**Speculative**, derived from Cell Bio 2025 Advanced Tools sessions + bioRxiv preprint cadence + active open-source projects.

### Microscopy hardware + acquisition

- **Lattice light-sheet 2.0 / adaptive optics** — speed + depth in live tissue
- **MINFLUX / DNA-PAINT** — single-molecule localization at sub-10 nm
- **Expansion microscopy variants** — iterative ExM, magnify, X10 ExM
- **Selective-plane illumination at organoid scale** — e.g., Janelia mesoSPIM
- **Cryo-CLEM and cryo-FIB-SEM** — connecting cell-bio fluorescence to structural biology

### Image analysis (open-source)

- **Cellpose 3** (Stringer / Pachitariu) — generalist cell segmentation foundation model
- **Stardist** — star-convex nuclear segmentation, MICCAI-adjacent
- **napari** + plugin ecosystem (napari hub, devbio-napari)
- **ZeroCostDL4Mic** — accessible deep-learning training for biologists
- **BiaPy / DeepImageJ** — deep-learning microscopy ecosystem
- **MorphoNet / CellPose 3 SAM-derivatives** — foundation-model cell segmentation

### AI / foundation models for cell biology

- **scGPT / Geneformer / scFoundation** — single-cell foundation models (cross-link to AACR 2026 virtual-cells axis)
- **Virtual-cell models** — CZI Virtual Cell Initiative, Tahoe-100M-class follow-ons
- **CellSAM / MicroSAM / nnU-Net derivatives** — image-foundation segmentation
- **Boltz-class structure prediction at protein-complex scale** — for organelle-machine modeling

### Cell-line / reporter / genome-engineering resources

- **CRISPR knock-in endogenous tagging at scale** — OpenCell-style atlases (Manuel Leonetti lab and follow-on)
- **Optogenetic / chemogenetic recruitment systems** — engineered tools for cell-bio perturbation
- **Synthetic-biology cell-state circuits** — bistable / tunable cell-fate constructs

### Single-cell + spatial in cell biology

- **MERFISH / seqFISH / Xenium at cell-bio resolution** — subcellular RNA localization
- **Spatial proteomics (CODEX / MIBI / IBEX)** — multiplex protein imaging at cell-bio panel size
- **Live-cell single-molecule RNA imaging** — MS2 / PP7 / SunTag updates

### Computational pipelines (cross-vault)

- **Bioconductor cell-image packages** — EuroBioC 2025 HistoImagePlot cross-link
- **Nextflow-based imaging pipelines** — Nextflow Summit overlap
- **napari + Galaxy image-analysis workflows** — GBCC 2025 cross-link
