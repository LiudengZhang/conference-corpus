## Venue

**IFEMA Madrid** (Feria de Madrid), Av. del Partenón 5, 28042 Madrid, Spain. The same congress center that hosts EHA and major European industry conferences; large enough for ESMO's ~30,000-attendee scale. [ifema.es/en/esmo-congress](https://www.ifema.es/en/esmo-congress).

ESMO rotates among major European host cities. The recent sequence: Madrid 2023 → Barcelona 2024 → Berlin 2025 → **Madrid 2026** → (cycle continues, future cities TBA). The Madrid IFEMA setup is well-known to attendees from 2023.

## Presidential Symposia — preview (historical pattern; 2026 slate not yet public)

ESMO Congress places its practice-changing trials in **three Presidential Symposia** rather than ASCO's single Plenary. Per the ESMO 2024 / 2025 pattern, expect:

- **Presidential Symposium I (typically Saturday afternoon)** — 4 LBAs, often anchored by a major breast-cancer or lung-cancer readout. ESMO 2025 placed DESTINY-Breast11 + DESTINY-Breast05 here.
- **Presidential Symposium II (typically Sunday afternoon)** — 4 LBAs, often lung / GI / GU. ESMO 2025 placed HARMONi-6 PFS + OptiTROP-Lung04 here.
- **Presidential Symposium III (typically Monday afternoon)** — 4 LBAs, often the cross-cutting / late-arrival slot. ESMO 2025 anchored this on ctDNA-guided trials (IMvigor011 MIBC, DYNAMIC-III CRC).

Each Presidential LBA is paired with a named discussant — typically the senior figure in the relevant European disease group. Discussant assignments publish with the program (~Jul/Aug) and finalize after the late-breaker deadline (Sep 8).

*The ESMO 2026 Presidential Symposia slate is **not yet public** and will not be until the Online Programme publishes. The table above describes the historical structure, not the 2026 content.*

## Embargo policy

**Critical differences vs ASCO:**

- ESMO **holds copyright** on accepted abstracts (per [ESMO 2026 abstract regulations](https://www.esmo.org/meeting-calendar/esmo-congress-2026/abstracts)). Abstracts cannot be made public prior to official publication.
- Regular abstracts are **not released weeks ahead** (the way ASCO releases titles in April and full text in May). Accepted regular abstracts typically appear online on the day prior to or the day of presentation.
- Late-breakers are **day-of** at embargo lift, timed to the start of the session.
- ESMO reserves the right to share material under embargo with journalists on the authorised ESMO media list who have agreed to respect the embargo policy.
- Abstract Book publishes **post-Congress, online only, as a supplement to Annals of Oncology** — every accepted abstract gets a citable Annals of Oncology DOI reference.

**Practical implication for this vault:** less pre-meeting reconstruction is possible than for ASCO. The pre-meeting build relies on (a) company topline press releases, (b) analyst previews (ApexOnco, OncLive, Targeted Oncology), and (c) the published program (~Jul/Aug). Most per-trial pages won't get populated until the meeting starts.

## Local notes — Madrid

- **Venue is in Campo de las Naciones (IFEMA),** northeast Madrid near Madrid-Barajas airport. Metro Line 8 connects directly to the city center / airport in ~15 min.
- **The same venue hosted ESMO 2023** — established logistics, large enough for the 30,000-attendee scale, multiple plenary halls.
- **Late October in Madrid:** typically 12–22°C, mild but variable; high probability of clear weather. No major Spanish public holidays in the meeting week.
- **Spanish oncology community is strongly represented in the program** — local moderator and discussant assignments to the Hospital 12 de Octubre, Vall d'Hebron Barcelona (~2.5h by AVE), IOB Madrid, La Paz, Clinica Universidad de Navarra are expected to be heavier than in non-Spanish-host years.
