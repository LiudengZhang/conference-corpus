## Track shortlist — most-loaded tracks

ASCO 2026 lists 23 oral abstract sessions; the following tracks are expected to dominate by abstract count and discussant attention:

### GI Oncology
- **Pancreatic:** Daraxonrasib LBA5 is *the* event. Plus KRAS-G12D early-phase combos (RMC-9805 / inhibitor + degrader combos previewed at ASCO GI Jan 2026).
- **CRC:** BREAKWATER follow-up (encorafenib + cetuximab + FOLFIRI in BRAF-V600E mCRC, mOS 30.3 vs 15.1 mo presented at GI in Jan); amivantamab OrigAMI-1 durability data; ctDNA-guided adjuvant trials.
- **Gastric/GEJ:** HERIZON-GEA-01 follow-up (zanidatamab + chemo ± tislelizumab vs trastuzumab + chemo — practice-changing at GI 2026); HER2-ADC updates.

### GU Oncology
- **Prostate:** PROTEUS LBA1 (perioperative apalutamide); PEACE-3 follow-up (enza + Ra-223); first-in-human actinium-225 PSMA (Fred Saad).
- **RCC:** LITESPARK-022 (adjuvant pembro + belzutifan, DFS positive); LITESPARK-011 (belzutifan + lenva vs cabo, 2L+); CheckMate adjuvant updates.
- **Bladder:** KEYNOTE-B15 (perioperative EV + pembro vs gem/cis — practice-changing MIBC); SWOG S-1602 BCG strain (Tokyo-172 noninferiority for NMIBC).

### Lung
- **NSCLC adjuvant / perioperative:** LIBRETTO-432 LBA3 (RET+, EFS), ALCHEMIST EA5142 (Abstract 8000, adjuvant nivo), CROWN 7-yr (Abstract 8502).
- **NSCLC 1L:** HARMONi-6 LBA4 (ivonescimab squamous), WU-KONG28 LBA8500 (sunvozertinib EGFR exon20), TRITON Abstract 8515 (STK11/KEAP1/KRAS IO-cold subset).
- **SCLC:** TRIPLEX LBA8005 (TRT + chemo-IO); DAREON-5 obrixtamig DLL3 bispecific.

### Breast
- **HR+/HER2-:** persevERA LBA1006 (giredestrant + palbo missed primary — preserves SOC), SERENA-6 PFS2 LBA1007 (camizestrant switch design follow-up).
- **TNBC:** ASCENT-04 PFS2 LBA1000 (sacituzumab govitecan + pembro 1L).
- **HER2+:** KN026-004 LBA660 (anbenitamab + nab-doc neoadj, China).

### Hematologic malignancies
- **Lymphoma:** frontMIND LBA7000 (tafasitamab + lena + R-CHOP, 1L DLBCL high-IPI — PFS positive HR 0.75).
- **Myeloma:** MajesTEC-3 follow-up (teclistamab + dara, 1–3L MM); RedirecTT-1 (talquetamab + teclistamab, EMD); CAR-T sequencing real-world data.
- **AML / MDS:** menin inhibitors (revumenib, ziftomenib post-ASH approvals); FLT3 / IDH updates.

### Melanoma
- **OptimUM-02 LBA9503** — IDEAYA / Servier darovasertib + crizotinib in 1L HLA-A*02:01-neg metastatic uveal melanoma. *PFS 6.9 vs 3.1 mo (HR 0.42, p<0.0001), ORR 37% vs 6%. Marlana Orloff presenting. NDA H2 2026.* First effective 1L regimen for mUM. Per IDEAYA April 21 release.

### Other tracks of interest
- **Sarcoma:** SARC041 LBA2 (abemaciclib, dediff liposarcoma).
- **GYN:** ovarian PARP / IO updates; endometrial dostarlimab / pembro follow-up.
- **Symptom science / GLP-1s:** ASCO press preview flagged GLP-1s in oncology and exercise / lifestyle research as 2026 emphasis areas.
