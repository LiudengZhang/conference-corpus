# ASTRO 2026

**68th ASTRO Annual Meeting** — Boston Convention and Exhibition Center (BCEC), Boston, MA · Sep 26 – 30, 2026. ~11,000 attendees, the largest radiation-oncology meeting in the world. 2026 theme: **"Data to Dialogue: Communicating Radiotherapy's Value to Advance Care."**

> **Status:** Scaffold — meeting is **~4.5 months out** as of today (May 11, 2026). Abstract acceptance notifications are scheduled to land **May 14, 2026** (per ASTRO 2026 Abstract Guidelines); regular-abstract embargoes lift in stages between then and the meeting, with late-breaking abstracts released on the day of presentation. This vault stands up the per-trial template and program-notes prep page now so the live-coverage scaffold is ready when the program grid and Plenary slate (3–5 top-rated abstracts) are released later in the summer.

## Why this is in the vault

ASTRO is the **radiation-oncology counterpart** to [ASCO 2026](../asco-2026/index.md). Same disease groups (prostate, lung, breast, GI, brain mets, head-and-neck), one modality over: where ASCO carries the systemic-therapy randomized phase 3, ASTRO carries the **locoregional control + technology** readout — SBRT fractionation, proton-vs-photon, hypofractionation, oligometastatic ablation, MR-linac adaptive workflows, FLASH-RT first-in-human, brain-mets SRS, and the **AI in treatment planning / contouring** secondary track that bridges to [RSNA 2026](../rsna-2026/index.md) and [MICCAI 2026](../miccai-2026/index.md).

ASCO 2026's Plenary trials (PROTEUS prostate, LIBRETTO-432 adjuvant lung, KEYNOTE-B15 perioperative bladder) all have ASTRO-side counterparts where the **radiation arm or radiation-modality question** is the contested variable. Cross-link discipline matters here — many "ASTRO trials" are NRG / RTOG-Foundation / EORTC / Canadian cooperative-group studies that ASCO discussants reference but never present in full.

**Format note (and the design decision behind this vault):** ASTRO is **primarily clinical, secondarily tools** — closer in shape to the ASCO vault than to AACR or RSNA, but with a meaningful AI / treatment-planning secondary track that warrants flagging. The per-trial template is the primary artifact. The AI-tools layer (auto-contouring, dose-prediction, synthetic-CT, MR-linac online adaptive AI, FLASH-dosimetry models) is captured **inside the relevant trial pages** when the model is named, with a cross-link table in `program-notes.md` pointing at the matching RSNA / MICCAI tool dossiers — no standalone `tools/` directory at scaffold stage. If post-meeting coverage warrants it, a separate `tools/` directory gets carved out.

## Program shape

ASTRO 2026 runs **Saturday Sep 26 (pre-meeting) through Wednesday Sep 30**, five days, BCEC. The structure (per the 2025 template + 2026 scientific-program page):

- **Saturday Sep 26** — pre-meeting refresher courses, workshops, residents' day; exhibit hall load-in.
- **Sunday Sep 27** — opening day. Presidential Address + named lectures. First scientific sessions. Exhibit Hall opens.
- **Sunday – Tuesday (Sep 27–29)** — full program days. Scientific sessions, oral abstract sessions, panels, Clinical Trials sessions, Science Highlights, Cancer Breakthroughs. **Plenary Session** (3–5 top-rated abstracts, unopposed) — typically Tuesday morning. **Late-Breaking Abstract (LBA) session** — typically Monday or Tuesday.
- **Wednesday Sep 30** — closing day, lighter program. Final oral sessions; residents' track wrap-up.
- **Exhibit Hall** — open Sun Sep 27 through Tue Sep 29 (closed Wed). Vendor demo programming concentrated here: Elekta Unity, Varian Ethos / ProBeam, ViewRay MRIdian, Siemens Healthineers, RaySearch, MIM Software, Limbus AI, AI-Rad Companion, plus auto-contouring / synthetic-CT startups.
- **~2,500+ abstracts** (proffered papers, posters, digital posters) — same scale as 2025.
- **Tracks dominating the corpus:**
  - **GU (prostate)** — SBRT fractionation (5-fx SOC consolidation post-NRG-GU005); proton-vs-photon; oligo-met / oligo-progression strategies; PSMA-directed re-irradiation.
  - **Lung** — SBRT for early-stage NSCLC; SABR-COMET-type oligo-met platforms; chemo-RT + IO sequencing (PACIFIC successor designs); proton thoracic (RADCOMP/PROTECT NSCLC arm).
  - **Breast** — partial-breast accelerated; hypofractionation regional nodal; proton-vs-photon (RADCOMP cardiac follow-up); skip-RT post-neoadjuvant.
  - **CNS / brain mets** — SRS fractionation, hippocampal-sparing WBRT, post-op cavity SRS, leptomeningeal.
  - **GI** — pancreatic SBRT, anal canal de-escalation, esophageal proton, rectal cancer non-operative / TNT.
  - **Head & neck** — proton (TORPEdO follow-up), de-escalation HPV+, re-irradiation.
  - **Heme** — Hodgkin / MALT / orbital RT, BCL/CD19 CAR-T bridging-RT.
  - **Technology / Physics** — **MR-linac (Unity, MRIdian) adaptive workflows**; **FLASH-RT** (FAST-01/02 follow-up, first-in-human in solid-tumor sites); **AI in treatment planning, auto-contouring, dose-prediction**; **proton arc therapy**; **carbon-ion**; **synthetic-CT for MR-only workflows**.
  - **Immuno-RT** — RT + IO mechanism + early-phase (NRG-LU007 / FORCE / WITH-RT type designs); abscopal-effect biomarker work.

## What's different from ASCO 2026

- **The locoregional question is the question.** Most ASTRO trials test a **modality, fractionation, or volume** variable on top of a (usually unchanged) systemic-therapy backbone. The reading-template lens shifts from "drug vs comparator" to "dose / volume / modality vs comparator."
- **Cooperative groups dominate the LBA slate.** Unlike ASCO (industry-sponsored Plenary), ASTRO Plenary trials are mostly **NRG Oncology, RTOG Foundation, EORTC, Canadian cooperative groups, NRG-led NCI-CTEP trials**. Sponsor cross-links go through cooperative-group identity, not just industry sponsor.
- **Technology vendors anchor the secondary narrative.** Elekta (Unity MR-linac), Varian (Ethos / ProBeam / Hyper Sight), ViewRay (MRIdian), Siemens (carbon-ion, proton), IBA, RaySearch — these are the JPM-equivalent commercial players for ASTRO. Track product-cycle announcements like JPM tracks pharma pipelines.
- **AI-in-planning is real but secondary.** The treatment-planning AI track (auto-contouring, dose-prediction, synthetic-CT, MR-only workflows, adaptive-replanning AI) is meaningful — typically 50–150 abstracts per year — but it sits underneath the trial corpus, not next to it. Cross-link aggressively to [RSNA 2026 tools](../rsna-2026/tools/index.md) and [MICCAI 2026 tools](../miccai-2026/tools/index.md), don't duplicate.
- **Drug names are not the index.** Modality + trial-acronym is the index. (PROTECT, RADCOMP, NRG-GU005, TORPEdO, FAST-02, SABR-COMET-10, PACIFIC-9, EMPOWER.)

## Cross-vault links

- **[ASCO 2026](../asco-2026/index.md)** — clinical-translation sibling. Many ASCO 2026 Plenary trials (PROTEUS prostate perioperative, LIBRETTO-432 adjuvant RET+ NSCLC, KEYNOTE-B15 perioperative bladder, ALCHEMIST adjuvant lung) test **systemic-therapy** arms on the disease where ASTRO is testing the **radiation** arm. Cross-link by disease group and by trial number / cooperative-group identity.
- **[ESMO 2026](../esmo-2026/index.md)** — European counterpart; some EORTC radiation trials present at both ASTRO and ESMO.
- **[SABCS 2026](../sabcs-2026/index.md)** — breast counterpart. RADCOMP cardiac follow-up + partial-breast / regional-nodal RT trials often dual-present.
- **[RSNA 2026](../rsna-2026/index.md)** — medical-imaging sibling. Cross-modality AI for **treatment planning** (auto-contouring, synthetic-CT, dose-prediction) overlaps directly with RSNA's diagnostic-AI track. Imaging foundation models surfaced at RSNA frequently get re-purposed for contouring at ASTRO.
- **[MICCAI 2026](../miccai-2026/index.md)** — methods-side counterpart for the segmentation / dose-prediction / adaptive-RT-AI literature. MedSAM, SegVol, TotalSegmentator-RT, and the auto-contouring lineage all originate at MICCAI and surface clinically at ASTRO.
- **[AACR 2026](../aacr-2026/index.md)** — radiation-biology side. NRG-led radiogenomics, immuno-RT mechanism, RT-DNA-damage-response biomarker work all surfaces at AACR before reaching ASTRO trials.
- **[JPM 2026](../jpm-2026/index.md)** — financing / commercial layer for the radiation-oncology vendors (Elekta, Varian / Siemens Healthineers, ViewRay, RaySearch, FLASH-RT players).
- **[Conferences timeline](../../timeline.md)** — ASTRO's position in the year's oncology cycle (AACR Apr → ASCO May/Jun → ASTRO late Sep → ESMO Oct → SABCS Dec → ASH Dec → JPM Jan).
