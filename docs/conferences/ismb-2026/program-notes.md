**Tag legend:**
- `[PROC]` — proceedings paper (peer-reviewed in *Bioinformatics*); has a DOI ~1 month before meeting
- `[COSI]` — COSI-invited talk; abstract-only submission, no DOI
- `[BOSC]` — talk in the nested BOSC 2026 program (Jul 14–15)
- `[TUTORIAL]` — Sunday Jul 12 tutorial (9 tracks, IP1–IP9)
- `[KEYNOTE]` — distinguished keynote
- `[?]` — placeholder; populated post-meeting

**Sparse-coverage flags:**
- **Accepted-paper list not yet public.** Proceedings notifications go to authors May 5, 2026; the *Bioinformatics* online supplement typically posts mid-June 2026 (~1 month pre-meeting). Until then, per-paper titles + authors are not extractable from a public source. Monitor: `https://academic.oup.com/bioinformatics` — ISMB 2026 special issue, expected Jun 2026.
- **Per-COSI speaker rosters trickle in.** Microbiome and EvolCompGen COSI pages already list committees; most COSIs publish full speaker rosters in the 4–6 weeks before the meeting. Recheck the COSI sites listed below in early-to-mid June.
- **Sunday tutorials (IP1–IP9)** have titles but no syllabi yet. Tutorial pages typically post 3–4 weeks pre-meeting.
- **Slide deposits.** No central ISMB Zenodo community. Bioconductor speakers will deposit to [zenodo.org/communities/bioconductor](https://zenodo.org/communities/bioconductor). Other slides ad-hoc; recheck individual lab sites post-meeting.
