# ISMB / ECCB 2026

**Joint ISMB/ECCB 2026** — Liverpool, scheduled July 12 – 16, 2026 (joint year).

> **Status:** Scaffold — meeting upcoming.

## Why this is in the vault

Direct extension of AACR's bioinfo / comp-bio / AI-methods axis. ISMB is where the methods underlying tools like CHIEF, GigaPath, Geneformer, scGPT, and Cell2Location get peer-reviewed and presented. The joint year with ECCB makes it the dominant comp-bio venue of 2026.

## Planned template

AACR template, with the **tools matrix** as the anchor — many ISMB papers will become new entries in the tools index, or update existing dossiers.
