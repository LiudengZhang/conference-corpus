## Venue + format

- **Venue:** Moscone West, San Francisco, CA (the standard ASCO GU venue; co-located overflow at the Marriott Marquis for press / sponsor booths in prior years, varies year-to-year).
- **Attendance:** ~3,500 total (in-person + virtual hybrid). Roughly 2,400 in-person + 1,100 virtual per ASCO registration patterns from prior years.
- **Format:** 3 days, Thu–Sat. Hybrid (in-person + virtual). Co-sponsored by ASCO, ASTRO (American Society for Radiation Oncology), and SUO (Society of Urologic Oncology).
- **Daily theme structure:** ASCO GU has historically organized days by disease group — Day 1 RCC, Day 2 urothelial/bladder, Day 3 prostate (the highest-volume group). 2026 followed the same pattern. ~3 oral abstract sessions + ~10 rapid-orals + multi-disciplinary panel discussions per day. ~1,200 abstracts total.
- **Plenary structure:** Unlike ASCO main, ASCO GU does **not** have a dedicated single "Plenary Session" slot — instead, the LBAs are distributed across the disease-group days. LBA418 / LBA417 / LBA630 / PEACE-3 OS are the four practice-changing slots.
