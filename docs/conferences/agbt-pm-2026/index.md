Clinical-implementation, translational-genomics, and precision-medicine dossiers from the **AGBT Precision Health Meeting** (a.k.a. **AGBT-PM**) — San Diego, CA, **September 14–16, 2026**.

> **Status:** Pre-meeting (T-4 months as of May 11, 2026). This vault is being scaffolded from the [official conference site](https://www.agbtprecisionhealth.org/), the [Inside Precision Medicine 10-year retrospective](https://www.insideprecisionmedicine.com/topics/precision-medicine/agbt-precision-health-inside-the-conferences-10-year-rise-and-2025-agenda/), and 2025 recap coverage. AGBT-PM, like the main AGBT General Meeting, uses a **partially-closed** abstract policy — concurrent-session abstracts are visible to attendees, not posted publicly — so the build approach is the same **"one degree removed"** sourcing used in the [AGBT 2026 vault](../agbt-2026/index.md): vendor PR, trade-press recaps, podcasts, society press releases, and PI-affiliated press.

## Why this is in the vault

AGBT-PM is the **clinical / translational spinoff** of AGBT. Where the [main AGBT General Meeting](../agbt-2026/index.md) is a platform-launch showcase (sequencers, spatial instruments, chemistry kits), AGBT-PM is where those platforms get used in patients: rare-disease diagnostics, newborn screening, MRD monitoring, liquid biopsy, biobank-scale population genomics, and pharmacogenomic implementation.

- **Downstream of AGBT 2026 platform launches** — Roche Axelios, Element VITARI, Ultima UG200, PacBio CiFi/SPRQ-Hq, Oxford Nanopore PromethION cfDNA/cfRNA all targeted clinical workflows in their pitches. AGBT-PM is where the *clinical evidence* (or lack of it) for those platforms gets aired.
- **Adjacent to [ASCO 2026](../asco-2026/index.md) and [ESMO 2026](../esmo-2026/index.md)** liquid-biopsy and MRD trial reads — AGBT-PM sits between technology meetings (AGBT, ASHG) and oncology meetings (ASCO, ESMO, SITC), and recurring presenters here often have read-outs at all three.
- **Complements [ASHG 2026](../ashg-2026/index.md)** — ASHG is the academic human-genetics venue; AGBT-PM is the clinical-implementation venue. Same investigators, different framing.
- **Pulls from [AACR 2026](../aacr-2026/index.md) liquid-biopsy posters** — Guardant, Natera, Personalis, Tempus, Foundation Medicine all show clinical-validation data at both AACR and AGBT-PM in a given year.

## Program shape (historical)

AGBT-PM has run a consistent 3-day format since 2016:

- **Opening pre-conference workshops** — skill-building / hands-on (rotation: variant interpretation, AI tools, regulatory pathways).
- **Plenary sessions** — invited speakers across rare disease, AI, therapeutics, population genomics, implementation.
- **The Great Debate** — a recurring AGBT-PM tradition: two sides argue an unresolved clinical-genomics question (recent topics: WGS-as-standard-of-care, AI in variant calling, return-of-results policy).
- **Concurrent abstract sessions** — selected oral talks (closed abstracts).
- **Storytelling / patient sessions** — a distinctive AGBT-PM feature.
- **Trainee / mentoring track**.

## Caveats

- **Pre-meeting forecast.** Everything in this vault except confirmed dates, venue, speakers, and committee members is reconstructed from 2025 program shape and is subject to change once the 2026 agenda is published.
- **Abstracts are closed-ish.** Like main AGBT, AGBT-PM does not post a public abstract book. Post-meeting build will rely on vendor PR, trade-press recaps, podcasts, and speaker tweets/blogs.
- **AGBT-PM is clinical, not technical.** Do not expect platform-launch announcements here (those happen at the General Meeting in February). Expect: trial read-outs, biobank progress reports, implementation case studies, payer/regulatory talks, and patient-family stories.
- **No public abstract submission deadline** has been surfaced as of May 2026 — historically abstracts close ~6 weeks pre-meeting (late July/early August expected for 2026).
