# Program notes — AGBT Precision Health 2026

Research notes underpinning the [AGBT-PM 2026 vault](index.md). Confirmed dates / venue, organizing committee, speaker lineup, source aggregators, and pre-meeting dead ends.

> **Status:** Pre-meeting (T-4 months as of May 11, 2026). This page captures what is publicly known *now*, and flags what is missing for the post-meeting build pass.

## Confirmed dates / venue

- **Dates:** September 14–16, 2026
- **Venue:** San Diego, CA (specific hotel TBD on the public landing page; the 2025 meeting was at **Loews Coronado Bay Resort**, and 2023–2025 all ran in greater San Diego — Loews Coronado Bay remains the working assumption)
- **Source:** [agbtprecisionhealth.org](https://www.agbtprecisionhealth.org/) and [agbt.org Precision Health page](https://www.agbt.org/home/home/precision-health/) (fetched May 2026)

### History / venue context

- **Inaugural meeting:** September 2016, **Scottsdale, AZ** — launched by Michael Talkowski, Eric Green, and colleagues to address the gap between sequencing-technology meetings (the main AGBT GM) and clinical-implementation venues.
- **Venue pattern:** Has rotated between Scottsdale and San Diego in past years; **2024 and 2025 were both San Diego**, and 2026 continues the San Diego run.
- **Calendar pattern:** Consistently early-to-mid September, 3 days.

## Program shape (historical, expected to carry into 2026)

Per [Inside Precision Medicine retrospective](https://www.insideprecisionmedicine.com/topics/precision-medicine/agbt-precision-health-inside-the-conferences-10-year-rise-and-2025-agenda/):

- **Opening pre-conference workshops** — hands-on skill-building (variant interpretation, AI for clinical genomics, regulatory pathway, returning results).
- **Plenary sessions** — across rare disease, AI, therapeutics, population genomics, implementation.
- **The Great Debate** — recurring tradition; two sides argue an unresolved precision-medicine question.
- **Concurrent abstract-selected oral sessions** — Cancer / Computational / Implementation / Discovery tracks (track names approximate).
- **Storytelling / patient-family sessions** — distinctive AGBT-PM feature.
- **Trainee mentoring track**.
- **Poster sessions** — abstracts visible to attendees only.

## Anticipated tracks and themes (forecast — revisit when full agenda lands)

Based on 2025 program and confirmed 2026 speaker affiliations:

1. **Rare-disease genomic diagnostics** — GREGoR Consortium read-outs; diagnostic-odyssey case series; rapid-WGS in NICU; long-read clinical sequencing for cryptic variation.
2. **AI in clinical genomics** — emphasis (per 2025 framing) on "post-hype, validated patient outcomes": variant calling, splice-AI–style prediction, foundation-model embeddings for phenotype matching, automated medical-record interpretation.
3. **Precision therapeutics & n=1 in-vivo editing** — patient-specific gene-editing case reports (CRISPR / base / prime); regulatory pathway for ultra-rare; ethics and access.
4. **Population genomics / biobank read-outs** — Regeneron GHS exome+, UK Biobank WGS, NIH All of Us, FinnGen, Genomics England 100K → NHS GMS transition.
5. **Clinical sequencing implementation** — eMERGE phase-IV; clinical-throughput at Broad; UK Genomic Medicine Service rollout; payer / reimbursement.
6. **Newborn / prenatal precision sequencing** — BabySeq2, GUARDIAN, NewbornsInSC; cell-free DNA prenatal beyond aneuploidy.
7. **Liquid biopsy / MRD** — Guardant, Natera, Personalis, Foundation/Tempus/Caris real-world performance reports (typically vendor-track, not a named-speaker session).
8. **Pharmacogenomics implementation** — CPIC guideline rollout; preemptive PGx at EHR scale.
9. **Regulatory-variant interpretation** — MPRA at scale (Ahituv), splicing prediction, non-coding variant prioritization.

> Themes are **forecast**, not confirmed. Revisit when 2026 agenda publishes (typically August, pre-meeting).
