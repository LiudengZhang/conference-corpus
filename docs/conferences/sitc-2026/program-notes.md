## Expected tracks (per SITC 2025 shape; speculative for SITC 2026)

SITC 2025 had concurrent sessions across the following themes, and SITC 2026 is expected to track closely (organizing committee continuity, similar abstract demographics). To be confirmed against the SITC 2026 program-at-a-glance once posted.

1. **Cellular therapies** — CAR-T (autologous + allogeneic + in vivo), TIL, TCR-T, NK-cell, CAR-NK, engineered Tregs, engineered macrophages
2. **Checkpoint resistance** — combinations to overcome primary / acquired anti-PD-(L)1 resistance; novel checkpoints (LAG-3, TIGIT, TIM-3, VISTA, BTLA)
3. **Bispecifics + ADCs** — T-cell engagers, NK engagers, immunocytokines; PD-1 × VEGF; ADC + IO combos
4. **Neoantigen vaccines** — personalized mRNA, peptide, DNA platforms; shared-neoantigen approaches; vaccine + checkpoint combinations
5. **Biomarkers / immune monitoring** — multiplex IF, spatial transcriptomics, TCR repertoire, ctDNA-MRD, single-cell immune profiling, cytokine panels
6. **Microbiome × immunotherapy** — FMT, defined-consortium products, dietary intervention, prebiotic / probiotic combinations
7. **Spatial-immune profiling** — TME spatial atlases, response / resistance classifiers, biomarker development substrate
8. **Innate immunity in IO** — myeloid reprogramming, macrophage repolarization, NK biology, innate-checkpoint blockade
9. **Toxicity management** — irAE biology, CRS / ICANS, steroid-sparing interventions
10. **Pediatric IO + rare cancers** — cell therapy in pediatric solid tumors, rare-tumor IO
11. **Regulatory science** — FDA / EMA framework for cellular therapy, vaccine, biomarker qualification (joint sessions with FDA officials are a SITC tradition)

## Anticipated big readouts (drug / cell-therapy side)

**All entries below are speculative**, sourced from sponsor pipelines + AACR 2026 echo + JPM 2026 strategy + ASCO 2026 preview. To be reconciled with the SITC 2026 program-at-a-glance and the JITC abstract supplement. Categorized by likelihood + impact.

### Tier 1 — likely headline tracks

1. **In vivo CAR-T first-in-human safety** — Capstan / Umoja / Outpace / Orna LNP-delivered constructs. First-in-human B-cell depletion kinetics + safety would define a new modality. The AstraZeneca / Capstan thesis from JPM 2026 (autoimmune-and-onc crossover) sets up SITC as the venue for the first-clinical-data readout.
2. **Solid-tumor CAR-T inflection** — multiple academic + biotech Ph1 cohorts in GBM, mesothelioma, GI cancers (Claudin18.2, GPC3, mesothelin). Whether any cohort crosses the response threshold that justifies a pivotal trial.
3. **Personalized mRNA neoantigen vaccines update** — Moderna mRNA-4157 (V940) in adjuvant melanoma / NSCLC / RCC; BioNTech BNT122 (autogene cevumeran) in pancreatic adjuvant + CRC; Gritstone shared-neoantigen platform CRC. The Wu Smalley lecture frames the whole field.
4. **Allogeneic CAR-T durability update** — Allogene / Caribou / Cellectis CD19, BCMA constructs. Off-the-shelf cell-therapy proof point — durability is the long-standing question.
5. **TIL therapy beyond melanoma** — Iovance Amtagvi cohorts in NSCLC + cervical; competitive set in HNSCC, NSCLC.

### Tier 2 — class status checks

6. **Bispecific durability** — BCMA × CD3 (teclistamab, elranatamab, linvoseltamab), CD20 × CD3 (epcoritamab, glofitamab, mosunetuzumab), GPRC5D × CD3 (talquetamab) long-term follow-up. Antigen escape and T-cell exhaustion as resistance themes.
7. **PD-1 × VEGF bispecific mechanism-of-action correlatives** — ivonescimab (Summit / Akeso) post-HARMONi-6 ASCO Plenary; AbbVie RC148; BMS pumitamig. The class-defining moment may have been ASCO; SITC is the venue for the biological why.
8. **LAG-3 class status** — relatlimab + nivo follow-on cohorts; novel LAG-3 antibodies in expansion. Whether LAG-3 holds beyond melanoma.
9. **TIGIT class status** — post-Skyscraper, the survivors. Roche tiragolumab subset rescue; iTeos / GSK EOS-448; Gilead domvanalimab durability. Whether any subset-defined cohort revives the class.
10. **Microbiome × IO maturation** — Vedanta, Seres, Finch defined-consortium products in checkpoint-refractory melanoma + RCC; academic FMT cohorts. Wargo's SITC 2025 keynote thread, now with randomized data.

### Tier 3 — early-phase candidates worth tracking

11. **TCR-T solid-tumor expansion** — Adaptimmune afami-cel post-approval cohorts; TScan TSC-100/101; Immatics IMA203 PRAME.
12. **NK-engager updates** — Affimed AFM13 + AFM24; Sanofi SAR / NK-bispecific platform.
13. **Immunocytokines** — Philogen IL2-targeted; Asher Bio IL-2v engineering; Mural IL-12 tumor-localized.
14. **ADC × IO combos** — EV + pembro (Padcev), Trop2-ADC + IO, T-DXd + IO — durability and safety updates.
15. **HPV-driven cancer vaccines + checkpoint** — ISA101 + pembro HNSCC; competitive set in cervical.

## Anticipated tool / method talks to watch

**Speculative**, derived from SITC 2025 methods sessions + AACR 2026 spatial-and-single-cell talks + active commercial platforms.

### Spatial-immune profiling

- **Akoya PhenoCycler-Fusion** — large-panel mIF in trial correlatives
- **10x Xenium / Visium HD** — spatial transcriptomics in pre/post-treatment biopsies
- **NanoString CosMx** — spatial profiling case studies in cell-therapy cohorts
- **Standard BioTools / IonPath MIBI-TOF** — high-plex spatial proteomics
- **Lunaphore COMET** — fast spatial-IF workflows; classifier development

### Single-cell + TCR

- **10x Chromium 5' + scTCR** — pairing antigen-specificity to transcriptional state in TIL and CAR-T products
- **Adaptive immunoSEQ** — clonotype tracking pre/post neoantigen vaccine
- **MiXCR / Mvision / Repertoire AI** — open-source and commercial TCR-repertoire pipelines

### ctDNA-MRD

- **Signatera (Natera)** — tumor-informed MRD in adjuvant IO + vaccine trials
- **RaDaR (NeoGenomics)** — tumor-informed alternative
- **Tempus xM / Guardant Reveal** — tumor-naive MRD comparators

### Neoantigen prediction

- **NetMHCpan / MHCflurry / Personalis NeXT / Gritstone EDGE / BioNTech iNeST** — pipelines underlying personalized-vaccine programs. Comparative-benchmark sessions likely (Wu Smalley substrate).

### Microbiome

- **Shotgun metagenomics + 16S** — stool and tissue panels in microbiome × IO cohorts
- **Metabolomics** — bile acid + SCFA panels paired with response data

### Image analysis

- **QuPath / HALO / PathAI / Stardist** — TIL counting, multiplex-IF segmentation, response classifiers

### Cytometry

- **Cytek Aurora spectral flow** — 40+ parameter immune-monitoring
- **Standard BioTools CyTOF Helios** — mass cytometry deep panels
