# BREAKWATER cohort 3 — Encorafenib + cetuximab + FOLFIRI in 1L BRAF-V600E mCRC

**Headline:** Encorafenib + cetuximab + FOLFIRI (EC + FOLFIRI) delivered superior response rates and an OS curve trending ~30 vs 15 months over FOLFIRI + bevacizumab in 1L BRAF-V600E mCRC. Establishes the regimen as the new 1L standard for this biomarker-defined subset and pushes the all-comer chemo era out of first-line BRAF-V600E.

## At a glance

- **Sponsor / PI / institution:** Pfizer (Seagen legacy program); presented by Scott Kopetz, MD Anderson
- **NCT ID:** NCT04607421
- **Phase / arms / N:** Phase 3, randomized open-label; EC + FOLFIRI vs FOLFIRI + bevacizumab (control); cohort 3 N ~360
- **Primary endpoint(s):** OS, PFS, ORR (BICR)
- **Indication / line:** Previously untreated BRAF-V600E-mutant metastatic colorectal cancer
- **Comparator:** FOLFIRI + bevacizumab (investigator choice mFOLFOX6 + bev permitted)
- **Session:** Day 1 (Thu Jan 8, 2026), CRC General Session, Moscone West

## Headline result

The January 2026 cohort 3 OS update showed median OS trending approximately 30.3 vs 15.1 months in favor of EC + FOLFIRI (HR ~0.55), with the OS curve still maturing. ORR was substantially improved over FOLFIRI + bev, and PFS hit the protocol-specified hierarchical bar. The differentiator vs FOLFOXIRI + bev is the neuropathy-free profile, given oxaliplatin avoidance.

## Mechanism / class

Encorafenib is a selective BRAF kinase inhibitor; cetuximab is an anti-EGFR mAb that blocks the EGFR feedback loop characteristic of BRAF-V600E CRC. The doublet was previously approved 2L+ post-BEACON CRC; BREAKWATER moves the regimen with chemo backbone to 1L.

## Discussant takeaway

Howard Hochster (Rutgers) framed the data as the permanent exit of 1L BRAF-V600E mCRC from the all-comer chemo era — biomarker-targeted triplet now reference frame, FOLFOXIRI + bev relegated to non-BRAF-V600E.

## Practice-changing verdict

**Yes.** NCCN guideline update expected through 2026; FDA already approved EC + mFOLFOX6 in 1L BRAF-V600E mCRC (Dec 2024 accelerated approval based on BREAKWATER ORR); cohort 3 OS data converts that into full approval support.
