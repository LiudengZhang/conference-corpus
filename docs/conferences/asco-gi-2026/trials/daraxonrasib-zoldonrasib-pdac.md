# Daraxonrasib + Zoldonrasib (RMC-9805) — RAS-class expansion in metastatic PDAC

**Headline:** Revolution Medicines' RAS(ON) program delivered the most consequential PDAC signal in a decade at ASCO GI 2026 — daraxonrasib (pan-RAS) + chemo combination updates, plus zoldonrasib (RMC-9805, selective KRAS-G12D(ON)) Phase 1 expansion with ORR 30%, DCR 80%, and 86% of evaluable patients with >50% ctDNA KRAS-G12D decline (39% with complete molecular clearance). Sets up the Rasolute-302 Phase 3 Plenary LBA5 readout at ASCO 2026 on May 31.

## At a glance

- **Sponsor / PI / institution:** Revolution Medicines; presented by Eileen O'Reilly (MSK) and Mark Sausen / Andrew Hendifar (Cedars-Sinai)
- **NCT ID:** NCT05379985 (daraxonrasib RMC-6236-001); NCT06040541 (zoldonrasib RMC-9805-001)
- **Phase / arms / N:** Phase 1/2 dose-expansion combined readout; daraxonrasib monotherapy + chemo-combo cohorts; zoldonrasib monotherapy at 1200 mg QD / 600 mg BID PDAC expansion (~50 evaluable PDAC pts)
- **Primary endpoint(s):** Safety, RP2D, ORR, DCR, ctDNA clearance
- **Indication / line:** 1L+ metastatic RAS-mutant PDAC (daraxonrasib); KRAS-G12D-mutant PDAC (zoldonrasib)
- **Comparator:** None (single-arm Phase 1/2)
- **Session:** Day 2 (Fri Jan 9, 2026), Pancreatic General Session, Moscone West

## Headline result

Zoldonrasib delivered an ORR of 30% and DCR of 80% in evaluable KRAS-G12D PDAC patients, with 86% achieving >50% ctDNA KRAS-G12D allele-frequency decline and 39% reaching complete molecular clearance — the first selective G12D(ON) inhibitor activity signal in PDAC. Daraxonrasib + chemo expansion data confirmed the durability profile underlying the April 13, 2026 Rasolute-302 topline (mOS 13.2 vs 6.7 mo, HR 0.40, p<0.0001).

## Mechanism / class

Daraxonrasib (RMC-6236) is a pan-RAS(ON) tri-complex inhibitor that traps active GTP-bound RAS in a non-productive complex with cyclophilin A. Zoldonrasib (RMC-9805) is a covalent KRAS-G12D(ON)-selective tri-complex variant of the same chemotype — first selective G12D drug to reach the clinic with PDAC efficacy.

## Discussant takeaway

Eileen O'Reilly framed the RAS-class signal as the inflection point for PDAC — the first credible molecular-targeted PDAC story since gemcitabine. Discussant framing of toxicity (rash, GI) and G12-subgroup parity will carry directly into the Rasolute-302 Plenary discussant slot at ASCO May 31.

## Practice-changing verdict

**Foundation, not yet practice-changing.** The Phase 1/2 expansion data here is the scientific premise for the Rasolute-302 Plenary readout at ASCO 2026 (May 31). If RASolute-302 reads as toplined, full RAS-class entry into 2L+ PDAC SOC is imminent; zoldonrasib then defines a G12D-selective second wave.
