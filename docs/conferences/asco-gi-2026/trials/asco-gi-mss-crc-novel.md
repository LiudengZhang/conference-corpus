# MSS-CRC novel combinations — synthesis dossier (ADCs, bispecifics, IO combinations)

**Headline:** ASCO GI 2026 surfaced incremental but not yet practice-changing activity in MSS metastatic CRC across three classes — EGFRxMET bispecifics (OrigAMI-1 amivantamab), PD-1xVEGF bispecifics in CRC follow-on (rilvegostomig and class-mates), and emerging ADCs. None displaces SOC alone; together they sketch the post-regorafenib / TAS-102 landscape for the ~90% of metastatic CRC patients without MSI-H, BRAF-V600E, KRAS-G12C, or HER2 amplification.

## At a glance

- **Sponsor / PI / institution:** Multi-sponsor synthesis — J&J (amivantamab / OrigAMI-1), AstraZeneca (rilvegostomig PD-1xTIGIT and class), various ADC sponsors
- **NCT ID:** NCT05379595 (OrigAMI-1); rilvegostomig CRC cohorts NCT05848739 / related; ADC trials sponsor-specific
- **Phase / arms / N:** Phase 1b/2 dose-expansion across cohorts; ~50-200 patients per readout
- **Primary endpoint(s):** ORR, DoR, PFS, safety
- **Indication / line:** 2L+ MSS metastatic CRC, biomarker subsets (EGFR-amp, MET-amp, post-chemo+anti-EGFR)
- **Comparator:** Single-arm vs historical reference
- **Session:** Day 1 (Thu Jan 8, 2026), CRC General Session + posters, Moscone West

## Headline result

**OrigAMI-1 (amivantamab in MSS / EGFR-amp mCRC):** durability update post-AACR 2025 confirmed activity in a biomarker-selected MSS subset, with response durability supporting the EGFRxMET bispecific class as a real option in EGFR-amplified or anti-EGFR rechallenge populations. **Rilvegostomig and PD-1xTIGIT class:** CRC cohort signals remain modest in unselected MSS but show signal in MSS subsets with high TLS / inflamed microenvironment. **ADC readouts:** HER2-low / TROP2-directed ADC follow-ups extend class read-through from breast and gastric. No single MSS-CRC asset declared the next 2L+ SOC.

## Mechanism / class

Three mechanism classes synthesized here: (1) EGFRxMET bispecifics (amivantamab) — co-engage EGFR + MET to block bypass-track resistance; (2) PD-1xVEGF / PD-1xTIGIT bispecifics — combine checkpoint blockade with anti-angiogenic or T-cell co-inhibitory release; (3) ADCs against HER2-low, TROP2, CEACAM5, B7-H3 in MSS CRC.

## Discussant takeaway

CRC discussants framed the MSS-CRC space as still defined by absence — no single class has yet declared itself the post-regorafenib / TAS-102 backbone. Biomarker enrichment (EGFR-amp, HER2-low, MET-amp) is the path forward; all-comer MSS CRC trials remain hard.

## Practice-changing verdict

**No — incremental.** No single MSS-CRC asset at ASCO GI 2026 reaches practice-changing threshold. Class momentum is real for EGFRxMET bispecifics; ADCs and PD-1 bispecifics remain Phase 2 stories needing randomized confirmation.
