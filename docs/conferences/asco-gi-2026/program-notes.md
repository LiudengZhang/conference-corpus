## Format note

Unlike ASCO main meeting, ASCO GI has **no formal Plenary slot**. Each disease group runs a General Session with the highest-impact orals (~3–5 abstracts) framed by a senior subspecialty discussant. The "headlines" of the meeting emerge organically from these — at ASCO GI 2026, the consensus headlines were:

1. HERIZON-GEA-01 (zanidatamab in 1L HER2+ G/GEJ) — Day 3
2. BREAKWATER cohort 3 OS update (BRAF-V600E mCRC) — Day 1
3. Daraxonrasib + RMC-9805 PDAC expansion — Day 2
4. REFOCUS lirafugratinib FGFR2 CCA — Day 2

Plus the **negative** signal from KEYNOTE-937 (adjuvant pembro HCC), which preserves SOC.

## Track-by-track retrospective

### GI Oncology — Colorectal + anal (Day 1, Thu Jan 8)

**Headline:** BREAKWATER cohort 3 OS update positions encorafenib + cetuximab + FOLFIRI as new 1L SOC for BRAF-V600E mCRC.

- **BREAKWATER cohort 3 (Pfizer/Seagen).** EC + FOLFIRI vs FOLFIRI + bev in 1L BRAF-V600E mCRC. Response rates higher with EC; OS trending positive but data immature (~30.3 vs 15.1 mo per earlier press; full mature data pending). Neuropathy-free profile vs FOLFOXIRI is the differentiator. NCCN guideline update expected through 2026.
- **CIRCULATE-NA (CCTG CRC.10 / NRG-GI008).** ctDNA-guided adjuvant design update. Co-presented by Alliance / CCTG / NRG cooperative groups; interim feasibility + randomization data.
- **ctDNA correlatives.** Two abstracts — (a) ctDNA doubling time correlation with recurrence timing in resected colon cancer, (b) presurgical ctDNA prognostic value. Feeds the maturation of ctDNA as a per-trial enrollment biomarker.
- **FOxTROT-2 (UK MRC).** Elderly perioperative colon cancer FOLFOX. Subgroup feasibility analysis.
- **OrigAMI-1 mCRC durability update (J&J).** Amivantamab (EGFR×MET bispecific) in MSS / EGFR-amp mCRC; durability data post-AACR 2025.
- **POD1UM-303 / EA2176 anal cancer (Incyte / ECOG-ACRIN).** Retifanlimab maintenance Phase 3 in advanced SCCA — possible first IO maintenance OS signal in anal cancer.

**Discussant framing (Day 1, CRC):** Howard Hochster (Rutgers) framed the BREAKWATER data as the moment 1L BRAF-V600E mCRC permanently leaves the all-comer chemo era. ctDNA-guided design framed by Aparna Parikh (MGH) as the path forward for adjuvant trial design.

### GI Oncology — Pancreatic, NET, hepatobiliary, small-bowel (Day 2, Fri Jan 9)

**Headlines:** RAS-class drugs become real for PDAC (daraxonrasib + RMC-9805). REFOCUS lirafugratinib defines next-gen FGFR2 selective TKI class for CCA. KEYNOTE-937 negative — preserves SOC for HCC adjuvant.

- **Daraxonrasib Phase 1/2 expansion (Revolution Medicines).** Monotherapy + chemo-combo update in 1L+ metastatic PDAC. Foundation for Rasolute-302 Phase 3 Plenary readout at ASCO main meeting May 31 (mOS 13.2 vs 6.7 mo, HR 0.40, p<0.0001 per topline April 13, 2026).
- **RMC-9805 / zoldonrasib KRAS-G12D PDAC (Revolution Medicines).** Phase 1 dose-expansion at 1200 mg QD / 600 mg BID. ORR 30%, DCR 80%, 86% of evaluable patients with >50% ctDNA KRAS-G12D decline, 39% with complete molecular clearance. First selective G12D inhibitor with PDAC activity. Sets up registrational design path.
- **KEYNOTE-937 (Merck).** Adjuvant pembro vs placebo post-curative-intent HCC. **Negative RFS** — preserves active-surveillance SOC. Reinforces that adjuvant IO doesn't extrapolate cleanly from advanced-HCC IO success (atezo-bev IMbrave150).
- **TACTICS-L (Roche / academic).** Atezo-bev vs TACE in intermediate-stage HCC. Longer time to treatment failure with atezo-bev. Challenges TACE-as-default in intermediate-stage; likely shifts patient flow toward systemic therapy earlier.
- **REFOCUS lirafugratinib (Relay Therapeutics).** ~100-patient Phase 2 in FGFRi-naïve FGFR2-fusion CCA. Durable responses, favorable safety vs pemigatinib / futibatinib. Best-in-class candidate for FGFR2-selective TKIs (sparing FGFR1-driven hyperphosphatemia / ocular AEs).
- **IDH1-mutant CCA combinations (Servier + multiple).** Ivosidenib + chemo / IO follow-up. Second-generation IDH1 strategies.
- **NETTER-2 follow-up (Novartis).** Lu-177 dotatate + LAR vs LAR in 1L GEP-NET. Earlier-line radioligand use confirmation.

**Discussant framing (Day 2, pancreatic):** Eileen O'Reilly (MSK) framed the RAS-class signal as the inflection point for PDAC — the daraxonrasib + zoldonrasib combination data being the first credible "molecular targeted therapy in PDAC" story since gemcitabine. Discussant framing of toxicity / G12-subgroup will carry into the Rasolute-302 Plenary discussant slot at ASCO May 31. Andrew Hendifar (Cedars-Sinai) co-anchored.

### GI Oncology — Gastric / GEJ / esophageal (Day 3, Sat Jan 10)

**Headline:** HERIZON-GEA-01 is the practice-changing readout of the meeting. Zanidatamab + chemo (Jazz Pharma) displaces trastuzumab + chemo as 1L SOC reference frame in HER2+ G/GEJ.

- **HERIZON-GEA-01 (Jazz Pharmaceuticals).** Phase 3 in 1L HER2+ locally advanced unresectable or metastatic gastroesophageal adenocarcinoma. Three-arm: zanidatamab + chemo vs zanidatamab + tislelizumab + chemo vs trastuzumab + chemo. Both zani arms showed statistically significant + clinically meaningful PFS improvement vs trastuzumab + chemo; the IO-containing arm showed OS gain. FDA submission triggered Q1 2026; NCCN guideline reconsideration through 2026.
- **ILUSTRO follow-up (Astellas).** Zolbetuximab (anti-claudin 18.2) monotherapy + combination durability. Extends label rationale; reinforces claudin 18.2 selection biomarker.
- **HER2 ADC esophageal (T-DXd, disitamab vedotin).** Durability updates in HER2+ esophageal / GEJ. Class read-through.
- **CheckMate-577 long-term (BMS).** Adjuvant nivolumab post-CRT in esophageal / GEJ. Long-term DFS / OS update confirms established SOC.

**Discussant framing (Day 3, gastric):** Yelena Janjigian (MSK) framed HERIZON-GEA-01 as the trastuzumab-displacement moment in 1L HER2+ G/GEJ. The discussant question is now sequencing (which HER2 agent in 1L vs 2L+) and biomarker stratification (IHC 3+ vs 2+/FISH+; PD-L1 CPS interactions). Manish Shah (Weill Cornell) on the claudin 18.2 / zolbetuximab discussion.

## Dead ends

- **Full slide decks for individual oral presentations:** ASCO Meeting Library publishes abstracts but slide decks are session-by-session and gated behind ASCO Virtual registration for many sessions. Trade-press summaries are the proxy.
- **Discussant slide content:** discussant commentary is typically not published — captured via VJOncology / OncLive video standups and ASCO Daily News editorial only.
- **JCO publication delay for individual abstract pages:** the JCO 4_suppl 2026 issue is the canonical citation source but individual DOIs were minted on the date of session; some abstracts published only as `meeting library` permalinks.
