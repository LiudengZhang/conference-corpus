# JPM 2026

Day-by-day digests and cross-day synthesis from the **44th J.P. Morgan Healthcare Conference** (San Francisco, **January 12–15, 2026**) — the largest healthcare investor conference of the year.

**Caveat:** Unlike the [Nextflow](../nextflow-2026/index.md) and [AACR](../aacr-2026/index.md) vaults (which have primary transcripts and abstracts), JPM is invite-only — there are no public transcripts. Every claim in these pages is **one degree removed from the room**, synthesized from trade-press reporting (STAT, Fierce Biotech / Pharma / Healthcare, Endpoints, BioPharma Dive, Evaluate, Pharmaceutical Technology, Clinical Trials Arena, BioSpace) and primary company press releases. Inline citations on every page so claims can be traced.

## At a glance

- **~$8.3B** in announced JPM-week deals (Pharmaceutical Technology)
- **$14.5B** Boston Scientific / Penumbra — only true megadeal (medtech, Day 4)
- **$1B** Lilly–NVIDIA AI Co-Innovation Lab — largest pharma–AI infrastructure commitment ever announced at JPM
- **Up to ~20** biotech IPOs forecast for 2026 if windows hold (HSBC's Jonathan Norris); <12 priced in 2025
- **No pharma megamerger** — the dominant week-end framing was "all the meetings, none of the megadeals"
