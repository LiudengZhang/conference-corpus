## Venue

**Stockholmsmässan**, Massvägen 1, 125 30 Älvsjö, Stockholm, Sweden. Sweden's largest exhibition and congress centre, ~10 km south of Stockholm city centre, served by Älvsjö commuter rail station (~10 min from T-Centralen). Hybrid format: in-person + EHA virtual / Learning Center. ~15,000+ attendees expected. Stockholm last hosted EHA in 2013; the venue rotates among Vienna, Madrid, Frankfurt, Amsterdam, Stockholm, Milan, Copenhagen on the EHA circuit.

The Stockholm venue gives the **Karolinska Institutet / Karolinska University Hospital** investigator network local-host prominence — relevant for hematopoietic stem cell transplant (Karolinska is a senior European HSCT center), gene therapy for sickle cell / beta-thalassemia (Karolinska is a Casgevy authorized treatment center), and MDS (the Nordic MDS Group has strong Karolinska representation).

## Track shortlist — most-loaded tracks

### Lymphoma (DLBCL, FL, MCL, CLL, T-cell)

The single largest disease axis at EHA.

- **DLBCL bispecifics — 1L combinations + 2-yr durability interim.** Epcoritamab (EPCORE NHL-2 / -3 / -5 interim), glofitamab (SKYGLO interim, SUNMO / STARGLO long-term), mosunetuzumab (FL → DLBCL extensions). EHA frequently hosts the interim readout; ASH 2026 (Dec) gets the longer follow-up.
- **frontMIND interim.** Tafasitamab + lenalidomide + R-CHOP in 1L DLBCL high-IPI. ASCO 2026 LBA7000 PFS+ likely; EHA reconciles the European subset; ASH 2026 long-term update expected.
- **POLARIX 5-yr OS update.** Pola-R-CHP vs R-CHOP in 1L DLBCL — long-term durability data point.
- **CLL — pirtobrutinib + venetoclax fixed-duration European cohorts.** BRUIN CLL-321 European subset; GAIA / CLL13 (German CLL Study Group) follow-up; FLAIR (UK NCRI) long-term; MAJIC.
- **Mantle cell + indolent.** MCL pirtobrutinib / brexu-cel long-term; FL mosunetuzumab + lenalidomide; FL CAR-T (axi-cel ZUMA-5 European cohort).

### Myeloma

Second largest disease axis. Bispecific / CAR-T crowd.

- **CARTITUDE-4 long-term + CARTITUDE-5 / -6 interim** (J&J/Legend cilta-cel frontline pivots) — the frontline CAR-T moment, EHA interim.
- **KarMMa-3 long-term + KarMMa-9 interim** (BMS ide-cel) — parallel frontline pivot interim.
- **MajesTEC-3 / -5 interim** (J&J teclistamab combinations) — bispecific + dara / pom / len frontline pivots interim.
- **MonumenTAL-3 interim** (J&J talquetamab pivotal).
- **RedirecTT-1 / -2** (dual bispecific BCMA + GPRC5D in EMD MM) — class watch.
- **IFM 2020-02 / HOVON-MM** — European cooperative-group frontline MM. IFM is a load-bearing European MM voice.
- **Allogeneic anti-BCMA CAR-T** (Allogene, Caribou, others) — manufacturing / off-the-shelf class watch.

The dominant clinical question across the track: **sequencing — bispecific-after-CAR-T vs CAR-T-after-bispecific**; multiple European real-world registries (EBMT, IFM) will speak to it.

### Acute leukemias

- **AML menin (revumenib, ziftomenib) post-approval European.** Both approved late 2024 / 2025 (FDA + EMA in 2025). Real-world European + combos with ven/aza, FLT3i, IDHi. Frontline pivots interim.
- **AML maintenance — European cooperative groups.** HOVON-AML and GMLL maintenance studies (venetoclax post-induction + others).
- **FLT3, IDH frontline.** Gilteritinib, quizartinib, ivosidenib, enasidenib, olutasidenib — frontline + maintenance pivot interim. EMA-relevant.
- **B-ALL CAR-T European.** GRAALL adult ALL pivot; brexu-cel European real-world (EBMT registry); pediatric updates.

### MDS / MPN

- **Imetelstat post-IMerge European real-world** (lower-risk MDS TI). Nordic MDS Group cohort prominent given Stockholm venue.
- **Luspatercept frontline ESA-naive European** (COMMANDS long-term European subset).
- **IDH inhibitors in MDS** (Servier).
- **Myelofibrosis** — momelotinib, pacritinib post-approval European; selinexor combos.
- **IPSS-M-guided transplant decisions** in the EBMT registry — load-bearing European data on MDS frontline transplant.

### Cell-and-gene therapy axis (cross-cutting)

EHA shares this story with ASH but adds the **EU regulatory and access** layer.

- **CAR-T 4–5-yr durability European cohorts.** EBMT registry data on axi-cel (Kite), liso-cel (BMS), cilta-cel (J&J/Legend), ide-cel (BMS/2seventy). Real-world durability + SPM signal.
- **Gene therapy for SCD — European real-world.** Exa-cel / Casgevy (Vertex/CRISPR) and lovo-cel / Lyfgenia (bluebird) European treatment-center early experience. EMA approval status; Karolinska is an authorized treatment center. Pediatric data.
- **Beta-thalassemia gene therapy.** Casgevy + ex-betibeglogene long-term European TI data — EHA's traditional strength on TDT.
- **In-vivo gene editing for SCD.** Beam, Editas, Verve, Prime Medicine pipelines. Class watch — early-phase.
- **Hemophilia gene therapy — European durability.** Roctavian (BioMarin val-rox) and Hemgenix (CSL etranadez) 2–4 yr European FVIII / FIX expression curves.

### Non-malignant heme

EHA strength. Proportionally larger than at ASH.

- **Hemophilia non-factor agents.** Mim8 (Novo, FVIIIa-mimetic post-FRONTIER), concizumab (Novo), fitusiran (Sanofi) — bleed-rate pivots and long-term European cohorts.
- **Red cell biology.** Mitapivat in SCD and thalassemia (RISE UP, ENERGIZE follow-up); etavopivat in SCD (HIBISCUS pivot update).
- **PNH.** Pegcetacoplan, iptacopan, danicopan — C5 vs C3 inhibitor head-to-heads / combinations.
- **iTTP / TTP.** Caplacizumab + rituximab follow-up European cohort.
- **Thrombosis & hemostasis.** Factor XI inhibitors (abelacimab, milvexian, asundexian) — post-OCEANIC / LIBREXIA / OCEANIC-AF data. Cross-link to cardiology readouts at ESC Aug 28–31, 2026.
