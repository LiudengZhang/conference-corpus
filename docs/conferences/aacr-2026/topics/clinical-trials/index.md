# Clinical Trials

**642 poster abstracts, ~294,000 words.** Session transcripts are deferred — CT minisymposiums are largely serial readings of the posters; the 294K-word poster corpus substantively covers the track.

## What's here

- **Phase I/II/III core sessions** — 191 posters across 8 in-progress / first-in-human minisymposium poster sessions
- **Late-Breaking Research: Clinical Research** — 66 posters across LBR Clinical 1-4
- **Biostatistics in Clinical Trials / Surgical Oncology** — 13 posters on design methodology
- **Biomarker-correlative CT posters** — 50+ Biomarkers Predictive of Therapeutic Benefit posters
- **CT-adjacent spillover** — 385 posters across 100+ subject-specific sessions (lung, breast, ADCs, CAR-T, etc.) whose abstracts mention Phase I/II/III or "clinical trial"

**Overlap with other topics:** 76 also in Single-Cell & Spatial Omics, 24 in Bioinfo / Comp Bio / AI Methods, 6 in Virtual Cells. 546 are unique to this topic.

## Session transcripts deferred

25 CT parent sessions were identified but not extracted. Rationale:

1. CT minisymposium talks at AACR are typically authors reading their own poster abstracts verbatim. The 294K-word poster corpus already covers the content.
2. Extraction requires Swapcard CAPTCHA handling + per-session navigation + Vimeo config capture; Chrome downloads were blocked during the extraction session.

If specific sessions are needed, the IDs are documented in `transcripts/clinical-trials/README.md` of the [aacr-2026 GitHub repo](https://github.com/LiudengZhang/aacr-2026). Tier-1 candidates:

- Clinical Trial Design Workshop: Real World Evidence
- Bayesian and Model-Assisted Designs for Early-Phase Trials
- Bridging the Gap: Multilevel Strategies to Expand Access to Cancer Clinical Trials
- New Drugs on the Horizon: Part 1 / 2 / 3
- Advancing Patient-Centered Clinical Trials
- Modernizing Clinical Trial Communication (NCI AI toolkit)
