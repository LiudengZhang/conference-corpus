# ROSELLA — Relacorilant + nab-paclitaxel in platinum-resistant ovarian cancer (post-bevacizumab)

> **Abstract:** LBA, Plenary I · **Session:** Plenary I, Friday April 10, 2026 (opening session, San Juan Convention Center) · **Simultaneous publication:** *The Lancet* 2026 Apr 10 — DOI:10.1016/S0140-6736(26)00462-9

**Headline:** Phase 3 OS win with mOS 16.0 vs 11.9 mo (HR 0.65, p=0.0004) — the first GR-antagonist Phase 3 victory in solid tumor and the cycle-defining readout of SGO 2026; Corcept's Lifyorli (relacorilant) was already FDA-approved March 2026, making this the simultaneous *Lancet* victory-lap.

## At a glance

- **Sponsor / cooperative groups:** Corcept Therapeutics · GOG-3073 / ENGOT-ov72 / APGOT-Ov10 / LACOG-0223 / ANZGOG-2221 (five-group multi-region design)
- **PI / presenter:** Plenary I LBA, Apr 10 (presenter TBD per SGO program)
- **NCT ID:** NCT05257408
- **Phase / arms / N:** Phase 3, 2-arm randomized open-label; ~381 enrolled (intermittent relacorilant + nab-paclitaxel vs nab-paclitaxel monotherapy)
- **Primary endpoint(s):** OS and PFS (dual primary)
- **Indication / line:** Platinum-resistant epithelial ovarian, fallopian tube, primary peritoneal cancer, post-bevacizumab
- **Comparator:** Nab-paclitaxel monotherapy
- **Session:** Plenary I (Day 1, Apr 10)

## Headline result

mOS 16.0 vs 11.9 mo (HR 0.65, 95% CI 0.51–0.83, p=0.0004) — a 4.1-mo absolute survival gain in a setting where mOS has stagnated at ~12 mo for a decade. mPFS HR 0.70 (p=0.0076). Benefit was consistent across pre-specified subgroups including prior bevacizumab exposure and number of prior lines. Simultaneous *Lancet* publication day-of-presentation.

## Mechanism / class

Relacorilant is a selective glucocorticoid receptor (GR) antagonist — first-in-class oral non-steroidal modulator that blocks cortisol-mediated chemotherapy resistance without affecting the progesterone receptor. ROSELLA is the first Phase 3 win for the GR-antagonist class in any solid tumor.

## Discussant takeaway

Plenary I framing reframed platinum-resistant ovarian as a setting where mechanism diversity (GR antagonism alongside ADCs and pembro combinations) is now generating real OS gains — the decade-long stagnation broke in early 2026.

## Practice-changing verdict

**Yes — already practice-changing pre-presentation.** Lifyorli (relacorilant) FDA approval came March 2026, one month ahead of SGO. The *Lancet* publication and Plenary I slot anchored rapid NCCN-guideline incorporation. Cross-tumor GR-antagonist read-through expected in breast and prostate.
