# IMGN853-0420 — Mirvetuximab + carboplatin → MIRV maintenance in PSOC (FRα+)

> **Abstract:** LBA, Phase 2 · **Session:** Saturday April 11 / Sunday April 12, 2026 · **Simultaneous publication:** TBD (Gynecologic Oncology)

**Headline:** ELAHERE moves earlier — mirvetuximab + carboplatin induction followed by MIRV monotherapy maintenance achieves 68% ORR in the maintenance phase and 63.9% in the PARPi-pretreated subgroup, justifying repositioning of mirvetuximab into platinum-sensitive ovarian (PSOC) including post-PARPi.

## At a glance

- **Sponsor:** AbbVie (IMGN853-0420; ELAHERE life-cycle program)
- **PI / presenter:** Apr 11–12 presentation (presenter TBD)
- **NCT ID:** NCT05445778 (IMGN853-0420)
- **Phase / arms / N:** Phase 2 single-arm; FRα-high platinum-sensitive recurrent ovarian (PSOC) patients receive MIRV + carboplatin induction → MIRV monotherapy maintenance
- **Primary endpoint(s):** ORR in maintenance phase
- **Indication / line:** Platinum-sensitive recurrent ovarian cancer, FRα-high (≥75% PS2+ IHC)
- **Comparator:** None (single-arm)
- **Session:** LBA, Day 2/3

## Headline result

81% of patients continued from induction to MIRV monotherapy maintenance. ORR 68% in maintenance phase overall, and **63.9% in the PARPi-pretreated subgroup** — the practice-relevant number, because it demonstrates ELAHERE retains activity after PARPi failure. Safety: grade ≥3 neutropenia 15%, blurred vision 10%, thrombocytopenia 10%; pneumonitis 15% over prolonged therapy (monitoring required).

## Mechanism / class

Mirvetuximab soravtansine (ELAHERE) is an FRα-directed antibody-drug conjugate with a maytansinoid (DM4) payload, FDA-approved 2022 for platinum-resistant FRα-high recurrent ovarian and full approval 2024. IMGN853-0420 is the AbbVie life-cycle pivot from platinum-resistant into platinum-sensitive.

## Trial design deep dive

**Study architecture:** Phase 2 single-arm, two-stage design — induction phase of MIRV 6 mg/kg (adjusted ideal body weight) + carboplatin AUC5 Q3W for 6 cycles, followed by MIRV monotherapy maintenance Q3W until progression or toxicity. Enrollment restricted to FRα-high (≥75% of viable tumor cells with PS2+ IHC staining intensity) platinum-sensitive recurrent ovarian cancer. No comparator. 81% rollover from induction to maintenance is a key feasibility metric demonstrating the regimen is deliverable.

**Endpoint definitions:** ORR in the maintenance phase by investigator assessment per RECIST 1.1 (primary, calculated from maintenance-phase start). Secondary endpoints include induction-phase ORR, DOR, PFS, OS, safety per CTCAE v5, and PRO/QoL. The maintenance-phase ORR primary endpoint is unusual — it isolates the monotherapy contribution after carboplatin clearance.

**Key subgroup analyses:** PARPi-pretreated subgroup (63.9% ORR) is the practice-relevant cohort — establishes ELAHERE retains activity after PARPi failure, the highest-need PSOC niche. FRα IHC intensity (PS2+ ≥75% vs higher cut-offs) reserved for ASCO follow-up. Prior-line and platinum-free-interval subgroups TBD in poster appendix.

**Comparator context:** No randomized comparator in this Phase 2, but the 68% maintenance ORR substantially exceeds historical PSOC maintenance benchmarks (~30–40% for chemo, niraparib/olaparib in unselected post-platinum). Pneumonitis 15% over prolonged maintenance is the key tolerability concern relative to PARPi maintenance.

**FDA implications:** Not registration-enabling on its own — supports a Phase 3 PSOC design (likely vs investigator-choice chemo or vs PARPi maintenance in PARPi-naive). The PARPi-pretreated PSOC niche may admit accelerated-approval pathway if Phase 3 is concurrent.

## Discussant takeaway

Per AbbVie press and SGO Meeting News coverage: the 63.9% PARPi-pretreated ORR supports moving ELAHERE earlier into PSOC, including in the post-PARPi setting where therapeutic options narrow rapidly. Pneumonitis signal over prolonged maintenance therapy is a key monitoring item.

## Practice-changing verdict

**Pending — supports Phase 3 design but not yet practice-changing on its own.** Likely to anchor a Ph3 maintenance design (similar to GLORIOSA framework) and inform NCCN PSOC sequencing. AbbVie life-cycle for ELAHERE depends on the PSOC move.
