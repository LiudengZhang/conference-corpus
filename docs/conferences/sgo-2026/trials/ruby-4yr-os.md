# RUBY 4-year OS — Dostarlimab + chemo in 1L advanced/recurrent endometrial cancer

> **Abstract:** Plenary III · **Session:** Plenary III, Sunday April 12, 2026 · **Simultaneous publication:** TBD (likely *NEJM* or *JCO* mature OS update)

**Headline:** Mature 4-year OS in the dMMR/MSI-H subgroup shows HR 0.32 — a 68% mortality reduction, with ~73% of patients alive at 4 years; MMRp subgroup HR 0.79 with 7-mo median OS gain. Confirms dostarlimab + carboplatin/paclitaxel as 1L SOC for dMMR endometrial cancer.

## At a glance

- **Sponsor / cooperative groups:** GSK · ENGOT-EN6-NSGO / GOG-3031
- **PI / presenter:** Plenary III, Apr 12 (presenter TBD)
- **NCT ID:** NCT03981796
- **Phase / arms / N:** Phase 3 randomized double-blind; 494 patients (245 dostarlimab + chemo vs 249 placebo + chemo)
- **Primary endpoint(s):** PFS (primary); OS (key secondary, mature 4-yr update at SGO 2026)
- **Indication / line:** 1L advanced (Stage III/IV) or recurrent endometrial cancer, all-comer with dMMR primary analysis subgroup
- **Comparator:** Placebo + carboplatin/paclitaxel followed by placebo maintenance
- **Session:** Plenary III (Day 3, Apr 12)

## Headline result

In the dMMR/MSI-H subgroup, 4-year OS HR 0.32 (95% CI 0.17–0.63) — 68% mortality reduction with ~73% 4-year OS in the dostarlimab arm. In the MMRp subgroup, HR 0.79 (95% CI 0.60–1.04) with 7-mo median OS gain — trend toward benefit. The dMMR survival curve is among the steepest in any solid-tumor Phase 3 mature OS readout. No new safety signals.

## Mechanism / class

Dostarlimab is a humanized IgG4 anti–PD-1 monoclonal antibody. RUBY is the cycle-defining mature OS confirmation of the chemo-immunotherapy 1L paradigm in endometrial cancer (alongside NRG-GY018 with pembrolizumab).

## Discussant takeaway

Plenary III framing: the dMMR OS curve flattens to ~73% at 4 years — exceptional plateau-like behavior consistent with durable immune control. Reinforces molecular triage (dMMR/MMRp split) as the cornerstone of endometrial therapy selection.

## Practice-changing verdict

**Yes (confirmatory).** Dostarlimab + chemo was already 1L SOC for dMMR endometrial post-RUBY interim; the 4-yr mature OS locks in the magnitude of benefit and rules out late curve crossing. NCCN endometrial guidelines reinforced.
