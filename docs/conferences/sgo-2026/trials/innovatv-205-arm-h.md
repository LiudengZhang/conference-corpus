# InnovaTV 205 Arm H — Tisotumab vedotin + carboplatin + pembrolizumab ± bevacizumab in 1L recurrent/metastatic cervical cancer

> **Abstract:** Phase 2 cohort · **Session:** Monday April 13, 2026 (cervical Plenary V) · **Simultaneous publication:** TBD (Gynecologic Oncology)

**Headline:** Tisotumab vedotin (Tivdak) combined with carboplatin + pembrolizumab ± bevacizumab delivers ORR 66% with median DOR 13 months in 1L recurrent/metastatic cervical cancer — moving TV into the 1L combination space alongside chemo-IO.

## At a glance

- **Sponsor:** Pfizer / Seagen · Genmab (innovaTV / Tivdak program)
- **PI / presenter:** Apr 13 Plenary V (presenter TBD)
- **NCT ID:** NCT03786081 (InnovaTV 205)
- **Phase / arms / N:** Phase 2 multi-cohort; Arm H = tisotumab vedotin + carboplatin + pembrolizumab ± bevacizumab in 1L recurrent/metastatic
- **Primary endpoint(s):** ORR, DOR
- **Indication / line:** 1L recurrent or metastatic cervical cancer
- **Comparator:** None (single-arm cohort)
- **Session:** Plenary V (Day 4, Apr 13)

## Headline result

Confirmed ORR 66%, median DOR 13 months in Arm H. Safety profile manageable and consistent with the known TV ocular and peripheral neuropathy AE profile, plus expected chemo-IO toxicities. The result positions tisotumab vedotin in 1L combinations, ahead of the historical 2L+ approval setting.

## Mechanism / class

Tisotumab vedotin (Tivdak) is an antibody-drug conjugate targeting tissue factor (TF, CD142) — a coagulation initiator broadly expressed on solid-tumor epithelium — conjugated to monomethyl auristatin E (MMAE) via a cleavable linker. FDA-approved 2024 (full approval) for recurrent/metastatic cervical cancer post-platinum/IO. InnovaTV 205 Arm H is the 1L move.

## Discussant takeaway

Plenary V framing: the 66% ORR + 13-mo DOR in 1L is competitive with chemo + bev + pembro alone (KEYNOTE-826), and supports a Ph3 head-to-head design that includes TV in the 1L cervical combination architecture. Anchors the cervical-cancer ADC class alongside sacituzumab tirumotecan (TroFuse-036) and IBI130 (anti-TROP2).

## Practice-changing verdict

**No (Ph2 cohort) — but combination-design-defining.** InnovaTV 205 Arm H informs a registration-enabling Ph3 design moving tisotumab vedotin into 1L recurrent/metastatic cervical. NCCN cervical-cancer sequencing implications pending Ph3.
