## Cancer-genomics-relevant watch list

Track headline talks across these sessions; populate `talks/<slug>.md` from each:

### Cohort plenaries (most likely S-track or plenary)

1. **FinnGen R13** — next release of the Finnish biobank, ~520k participants expected, cancer-relevant pheWAS + drug-target validation continues to be ESHG-anchor content. *Expected per FinnGen public release cadence.*
2. **UK Biobank WGS + proteomics cancer overlay** — 500k WGS released 2023–24, multi-omics including Olink proteomics; expected cancer-PRS + hereditary-cancer prevalence updates. *Expected per UKB Showcase release pattern.*
3. **Generation R** — Erasmus MC Rotterdam pediatric / early-life cohort; less cancer-direct but methods bleed into developmental cancer-predisposition.
4. **GeneRISK** — Finnish polygenic-risk return-of-results pilot (FIMM / Univ. of Helsinki / Perola / Widén / Ripatti); cancer-PRS subset (breast / prostate / colorectal) included. First multi-year return-of-PRS-to-patients dataset including cancer endpoints. *Expected per GeneRISK public timeline.*
5. **Estonian Biobank cancer module** — Univ. of Tartu / Mägi / Metspalu group; pan-cancer germline-somatic on national cohort.
6. **deCODE pan-cancer germline update** — deCODE genetics; historically high large-effect cancer-predisposition variant discovery.
7. **GBMI / cross-biobank meta-analyses** — Global Biobank Meta-analysis Initiative cancer cross-biobank meta-analyses (cancer-PRS cross-ancestry transferability).
8. **Genomics England / NHS GMS** — UK 100k follow-on + NHS Genomic Medicine Service readouts; cancer-WGS NHS pathway data.

### Cancer-genetics symposia (S-track)

1. **Rare Cancers symposium** — overlaps AACR Rare Cancers track. European rare-cancer registries (EURACAN) anchor this. *Expected per public Programme-at-a-Glance "rare cancers" S-track entry.*
2. **Cancer Mechanisms symposium** — mechanistic cancer-genetics; overlaps AACR Annual Meeting mechanism sessions. *Expected per public Programme-at-a-Glance.*
3. **Polygenic Scores symposium** — cancer-PRS subset: breast (Mavaddat / Pharoah), prostate (Eeles / Schumacher), colorectal (Houlston / Tomlinson), lung. Cross-ancestry transferability + clinical-utility framing.

### Hereditary cancer (S/C track)

1. **Hereditary breast / ovarian cancer panel-design** — GC-HBOC (German Consortium for Hereditary Breast and Ovarian Cancer), HEBON (Dutch), SweBCG (Swedish), CanRisk (UK risk-prediction model). Pan-European panel harmonization is a recurring theme.
2. **Lynch syndrome / colorectal cancer** — InSiGHT consortium + European Lynch registries; immune-checkpoint MMR-deficiency framing.
3. **Li-Fraumeni / TP53 mosaicism** — methods bleed into cancer-mosaicism.
4. **Pediatric cancer predisposition** — overlaps with ASHG Mendelian + rare-disease tracks.

### Statistical-genetics (cancer subset)

1. **Cancer-PRS clinical utility** — trial-stratification feasibility, cross-ancestry transferability.
2. **Clonal hematopoiesis at population scale (CHIP)** — UKB + FinnGen CHIP overlays; bridges to EHA 2026 (same week!) and ASH 2026 MN-predisposition.

### Pharmacogenomics in oncology

1. **Ubiquitous-PGx readouts** — LUMC / Swen / van der Wouden; DPYD-guided 5-FU dosing real-world Dutch + Nordic data; UGT1A1-guided irinotecan; TPMT-guided thiopurines; CYP2D6-guided tamoxifen.
2. **Nordic PGx registries** — Karolinska / Uppsala oncology-drug genotyping rollouts.

### Methods / tools

1. **Foundation models for variant effect (E-track AI in Functional Genomics + S-track polygenic-scores spillover)** — AlphaMissense follow-ons, ESM / EVO genomic models, RNA-LM successors.
2. **Long-read clinical sequencing (E-track Long-read sequencing)** — PacBio HiFi + ONT R10 clinical-grade pipelines; European clinical-genome programs (Genomics England, NHS GMS, French Plan France Médecine Génomique).
3. **Episignatures (I-track)** — methylation-based classification for cancer-predisposition syndromes; overlap with cancer tumor-classification methylation panels (DKFZ classifier).
4. **Pangenome / HPRC** — clinical applications of pangenome reference; European pangenome-reference initiative if presented.
5. **AI dysmorphology (I-track Dysmorphology, two parallel sessions)** — Face2Gene successors, DeepGestalt-style models; indirect cancer-predisposition syndrome recognition.

### ELSI / ELPAG

1. **EU AI Act overlay on clinical genomics (E-track ELSI in AI Genomics)** — first major meeting where the EU AI Act clinical-deployment framing for foundation models is explicit.
2. **GDPR + cross-border biobanking** — recurring; relevance to cancer-cohort data sharing across European national systems.
3. **Return of polygenic risk** — pairs with GeneRISK cohort talk.
