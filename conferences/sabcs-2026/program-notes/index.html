    Program notes — SABCS 2026¶

    Internal pre-meeting working file. Tracks, GS preview, expected late-breakers, anticipated big readouts, key dates. Status: ~7 months pre-meeting (as of May 11, 2026). Almost everything below is speculative until the SABCS program drops in Oct–Nov 2026. Flagged accordingly.

    Key dates¶

    Date Event Notes
    May 29, 2026 McGuire Award nominations deadline 11:59 PM CST
    Jun 1, 2026 Housing registration opens sabcs.org
    Jul 13, 2026 Abstract submission deadline 11:59 PM CT; regular abstracts
    Sep – Oct 2026 Late-breaking abstract acceptance window Late-breakers typically due ~Oct 1
    Oct – Nov 2026 SABCS program release Session grid, GS slate, named lecturers

    Sources: SABCS Events page, SABCS 2026 home, AACR SABCS 2026 landing.

    Venue & logistics¶

    • Henry B. Gonzalez Convention Center, 900 E. Market St, San Antonio, TX 78205.
    • ~10,000 attendees from >105 countries.
    • Co-directors: Carlos L. Arteaga (UT Southwestern), Virginia G. Kaklamani (UT Health San Antonio). Program Director: Kate Lathrop.
    • Co-organizers: AACR + UT Health San Antonio + UT Southwestern.
    • Virtual streaming and on-demand replay typically available with registration.

    Format & embargo¶

    SABCS sessions historically use:

    • General Sessions (GS1–GS6, two per day × four days = up to 8 GS sessions) — the practice-changing slots, 5–6 abstracts each, with a session-level discussant / chair framing.
    • Mini-Symposia (MS1–MS6+) — translational/basic-science thematic sessions, 6–8 short talks each.
    • Spotlight Discussions / Rapid-Fire Orals (RF1–RF…) — shorter slots.
    • Ongoing Trials (OT) posters — pipeline tracking.
    • Posters (PD / P1–P6) — translational, clinical, prevention, basic biology.
    • Plenary / named lectures: William L. McGuire Memorial Lecture, AACR Distinguished Lectureship in Breast Cancer Research, Brinker Awards (basic + clinical), Susan G. Komen leadership awards.
    • Educational sessions, forums, case discussions for practicing clinicians.

    Embargo policy: SABCS abstracts publish to the Cancer Research online supplement and become citable on the day of presentation. Late-breakers in particular embargo until session start (typically 7:00 AM CT day of). Sponsor topline press releases frequently precede the embargo. Detailed 2026 embargo timing not yet posted publicly — to be confirmed once the press-program page goes live (typically Nov 2026).

    Anticipated big readouts — pre-meeting watch list¶

    All entries below are speculative, sourced from sponsor 2026 readout guidance (JPM 2026, Q1/Q2 earnings calls), prior-year SABCS slot patterns, ongoing-trial timelines, and analyst preview material. Trial slot assignment to a GS number is unknown until program release. Update post-program.

    Top 5 anticipated practice-changing readouts¶

    1. DESTINY-Breast09 (HER2+ 1L MBC, T-DXd ± pertuzumab vs THP) — Daiichi/AZ. PFS positive per topline (timing per Daiichi/AZ Q1 2026 commentary). The headline of the meeting if PFS magnitude is large — would displace THP as 1L HER2+ SOC and reset the trastuzumab pertuzumab paradigm in place since CLEOPATRA (2012). Cross-link to DESTINY-Breast03 SABCS 2021 (2L reset) and DESTINY-Breast06 ASCO 2024 (HR+/HER2-low/ultralow). [Pre-embargo, sponsor-guided.]
    2. HER2+ adjuvant DESTINY series (DESTINY-Breast05 / -11). DB-05 (T-DXd vs T-DM1 post-neoadj residual) is the KATHERINE-replacement question; DB-11 (T-DXd → THP neoadj vs ddAC-THP) is the HER2+ neoadj reset. Either positive readout reshapes the HER2+ early-stage SOC. Timing risk: DB-11 may slip to 2027. [Pre-embargo, sponsor-guided.]
    3. NATALEE long-term + monarchE 7-yr OS — CDK4/6 adjuvant face-off. monarchE has the brand and the longer follow-up; NATALEE has the broader stage II eligibility and arguably more tolerable schedule. The 2026 readout pair starts ranking these against each other in OS terms (not just iDFS). Practice impact: NCCN adjuvant CDK4/6 stratification by stage / nodal status. [Pre-embargo; topline OS not yet released for either.]
    4. KEYNOTE-756 EFS (HR+ high-risk EBC neoadj pembro + chemo) — MSD. The pCR readout at SABCS 2023 was positive but modest; the practice-changing test is whether pCR translates to EFS. If positive, extends pembro from TNBC neoadj (KEYNOTE-522 SOC) into HR+ high-risk EBC — a much larger population. Cross-link to CheckMate 7FL EFS readout in parallel. [Pre-embargo, follow-up timing per MSD guidance.]
    5. Next-gen oral SERD class definition: EMBER-3 mature + VERITAC-2 (vepdegestrant PROTAC) + persevERA OS context. EMBER-3 mature OS (imlunestrant + abema in ESR1-mut 2L) sets the ceiling; VERITAC-2 validates the PROTAC class; persevERA OS context-sets the SERD-monotherapy ceiling. The composite tells the field whether SERDs become a 1L SOC, stay 2L+ ESR1-mut, or remain a niche. [Pre-embargo; mixed sponsor guidance.]

    Sponsor map — who's on the SABCS 2026 stage¶

    The major breast pipelines expected to anchor 2026 readouts:

    • AstraZeneca + Daiichi Sankyo — DESTINY-Breast series (T-DXd: DB-09 1L, DB-05/-11 adjuvant, DB-12 brain mets), TROPION-Breast (datopotamab), CAPItello-291 (capivasertib), camizestrant (SERENA series → SABCS readouts on switch / 2L follow-up), Enhertu lifecycle.
    • Lilly — monarchE 7-yr (abemaciclib), EMBER-3/-4 (imlunestrant), postMONARCH, NATALEE-competitor positioning, CDK2/4 selectives in early phase.
    • Pfizer — vepdegestrant (Arvinas-partnered PROTAC, VERITAC-2), HER2CLIMB-05/-02 (tucatinib post-Seagen acquisition), elacestrant (Stemline → Menarini) class competition.
    • Roche / Genentech — INAVO series (inavolisib, PIK3CA-mut), giredestrant (persevERA OS), tucatinib competitive positioning, T-DM1 lifecycle in DB-05 context.
    • MSD — KEYNOTE-756 EFS (HR+ EBC neoadj), KEYNOTE-522 long-term (TNBC neoadj SOC), pembro combinations.
    • Gilead — ASCENT-03/-04 PFS2 (sacituzumab govitecan TROP2-ADC), TROPiCS-02 OS update.
    • Novartis — NATALEE long-term (ribociclib adjuvant), Kisqali franchise lifecycle.
    • Daiichi (separately) / BMS / Menarini-Stemline / Arvinas / Olema — class competition across SERDs, ADCs, AKT-i, PI3K-i, PROTACs.

    JPM 2026 day-by-day digests in ../jpm-2026/ flagged most of these toplines as Q4 2026 / SABCS 2026 events in the January 2026 presentations.

    Tracks expected to dominate the 2026 corpus¶

    HR+/HER2- MBC¶

    • Next-gen oral SERDs — camizestrant, imlunestrant, vepdegestrant (PROTAC), elacestrant — class definition vs CDK4/6 + AI SOC.
    • Post-CDK4/6 sequencing — postMONARCH, INAVO121, CAPItello-291 OS, ESR1-mut switch designs.
    • ESR1-mut detection — ctDNA-guided switch (SERENA-6 paradigm extended into 2L).

    HER2+ MBC and EBC¶

    • 1L MBC reset — DESTINY-Breast09 (THP displacement).
    • Adjuvant — DESTINY-Breast05 (T-DM1 niche), DESTINY-Breast11 (neoadj reset).
    • Brain mets — DESTINY-Breast12 update, HER2CLIMB-05.
    • De-escalation — PHERGain, CompassHER2 chemo-free / reduced-chemo regimens.

    TNBC¶

    • TROP2-ADC class — sacituzumab (ASCENT-04 PFS2, ASCENT-03), datopotamab (TROPION-Breast02).
    • Adjuvant post-KEYNOTE-522 — OptimICE-RD, A-BRAVE final, residual-disease intensification.
    • AR+ / LAR subtype — early-phase AR-targeted programs.

    Early-stage adjuvant / neoadjuvant¶

    • CDK4/6 adjuvant — monarchE 7-yr, NATALEE long-term.
    • Neoadjuvant IO in HR+ EBC — KEYNOTE-756 EFS, CheckMate 7FL EFS.
    • ctDNA-MRD-guided adjuvant — DARE, LEADER, c-TRAK-TN.

    Biomarker development¶

    • HER2-low / HER2-ultralow — assay standardization, T-DXd eligibility.
    • ctDNA MRD — assay performance, intervention triggering, FDA / NCCN posture.
    • PIK3CA / AKT1 / PTEN — co-mutation patterns, AKT-i eligibility.

    Brain metastases¶

    • DESTINY-Breast12 confirmatory; HER2CLIMB-05 tucatinib + ADC; intrathecal regimens; leptomeningeal disease early-phase.

    Survivorship / prevention / disparities¶

    • POSITIVE long-term (pregnancy interruption, fertility, ET resumption durability).
    • GLP-1 in breast cancer — obesity / metabolic intersection; cross-link to ASCO 2026 GLP-1 press preview theme.
    • Chemoprevention long-term — anastrozole / tamoxifen extended follow-up.
    • Racial disparities in ADC access / outcomes.
