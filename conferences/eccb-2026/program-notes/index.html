    ECCB 2026 — Program Notes (working file)¶

    Internal-use working file. Source for the bulk tool-page generation pass that will run after the meeting closes Sep 4, 2026 and the Bioinformatics OUP supplement is fully online. Not for end-reader consumption — formatting is utilitarian. Compiled from the public ECCB 2026 web pages (eccb2026.org), fetched 2026-05-11 (~16 weeks pre-meeting).

    Tag legend: - [PROC] — proceedings paper (peer-reviewed in Bioinformatics OUP supplement); has a DOI when supplement posts - [HIGHLIGHT] — highlight talk; recently published research (post Mar 1, 2025); preprint or journal DOI exists - [ELIXIR] — talk in the ELIXIR Track (community / infrastructure stream embedded in main program) - [INDUSTRY] — Industry Track talk (one session, Monday) - [FOCUS] — Focus session talk (4 sessions across Tue + Wed; topical themes) - [TUTORIAL] — Thursday Sep 3 tutorial / workshop - [SCS] — ISCB Student Council Symposium (Thu Sep 3) - [COMMUNITIES] — Communities Day satellite (Fri Sep 4) - [KEYNOTE] — distinguished keynote - [?] — placeholder; populated post-meeting

    Sparse-coverage flags: - Accepted proceedings list not yet public. Proceedings notifications went to authors mid-May 2026 (current cycle). The Bioinformatics OUP supplement typically posts in late-July or August 2026 (~3–6 weeks before the meeting). Until then, per-paper titles + authors are not extractable from a public source. Monitor: academic.oup.com/bioinformatics — ECCB 2026 special issue, expected Jul–Aug 2026. - Highlight-talk acceptances trickle in. Submissions closed Apr 20, 2026; acceptance notifications go to authors early-June 2026. Recheck the highlight-talks page in mid-June. - ELIXIR Track program not yet public. Four ELIXIR Track sessions are scheduled (3 on Mon Aug 31, 1 on Tue Sep 1, 1 on Wed Sep 2 — total 5 by the day-by-day program, but consistently described as "ELIXIR Track" across 3 days). Session topics typically firm up in June via ELIXIR Europe's events calendar. - Tutorial / workshop list not yet public. Tutorials & Workshops chairs (Patricia Palagi + Diana Marek, SIB) typically post the tutorial list 6–8 weeks pre-meeting (late-June / early-July). - Communities Day (Sep 4) satellite list not yet public. SIB Communities organizes independent events; each Community typically posts its own program 4–6 weeks pre-meeting on the SIB Communities pages. - Slide deposits. No central ECCB Zenodo community historically. Bioconductor speakers deposit to zenodo.org/communities/bioconductor. SIB speakers may deposit to a SIB Zenodo community. Others ad-hoc. - Recordings. ECCB historically uneven on recordings. ISCB Nucleus may host some content; recheck post-meeting.

    Verified high-level facts (2026-05-11)¶

    • Conference: ECCB 2026 — 25th European Conference on Computational Biology
    • Dates: Mon Aug 31 – Fri Sep 4, 2026
    • Venue: Geneva, Switzerland (specific venue not yet named on public site; SIB-organized)
    • Theme: "Biodiversity, AI & Health: computational biology to address the challenges of our time"
    • Organizer: SIB Swiss Institute of Bioinformatics
    • Joint year? NO. This is ECCB-only. ISMB/ECCB joint year was 2025 (Liverpool, Jul 20–24). ISMB 2026 is a separate event: Washington DC, Jul 12–16. We treat ECCB 2026 and ISMB 2026 as sibling vaults, not merged.
    • Scale: ~1,000–2,000 attendees historical baseline (between EuroBioC's ~250 and ISMB's ~3,000)
    • Proceedings: Online-only OUP Bioinformatics special issue. CC-BY. $715 USD APC. Submissions closed; notifications mid-May 2026. Author registration deadline Jun 30, 2026. Expected supplement online Jul–Aug 2026.
    • Highlight talks: Window closed Apr 20, 2026. Notifications early-June 2026. Eligibility: published post Mar 1, 2025.
    • Posters: Same submission cycle as highlight talks (closed Apr 20, 2026; notifications early-June 2026).
    • Distinguished Keynotes: 4 confirmed (Farrar, Malaspinas, Quake, Walczak)
    • Scientific Areas: 5 (Genomics, Transcriptomics, Proteins/Structural Biology, Systems Biology, Biodiversity/Sustainability)
    • ELIXIR Track: embedded in Mon/Tue/Wed main program (count ≈4 sessions)
    • Tutorials & Workshops: Thu Sep 3 (count not yet public; SIB Training chairs)
    • ISCB Student Council Symposium: Thu Sep 3
    • Communities Day: Fri Sep 4 — independent SIB Communities satellites
