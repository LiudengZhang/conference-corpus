    ISMB 2026 — Program Notes (working file)¶

    Internal-use working file. Source for the bulk tool-page generation pass that will run immediately after the meeting closes Jul 16, 2026. Not for end-reader consumption — formatting is utilitarian. Compiled from the public ISMB 2026 web pages and the abridged-agenda PDF, fetched 2026-05-11 (~9 weeks pre-meeting).

    Tag legend: - [PROC] — proceedings paper (peer-reviewed in Bioinformatics); has a DOI ~1 month before meeting - [COSI] — COSI-invited talk; abstract-only submission, no DOI - [BOSC] — talk in the nested BOSC 2026 program (Jul 14–15) - [TUTORIAL] — Sunday Jul 12 tutorial (9 tracks, IP1–IP9) - [KEYNOTE] — distinguished keynote - [?] — placeholder; populated post-meeting

    Sparse-coverage flags: - Accepted-paper list not yet public. Proceedings notifications go to authors May 5, 2026; the Bioinformatics online supplement typically posts mid-June 2026 (~1 month pre-meeting). Until then, per-paper titles + authors are not extractable from a public source. Monitor: https://academic.oup.com/bioinformatics — ISMB 2026 special issue, expected Jun 2026. - Per-COSI speaker rosters trickle in. Microbiome and EvolCompGen COSI pages already list committees; most COSIs publish full speaker rosters in the 4–6 weeks before the meeting. Recheck the COSI sites listed below in early-to-mid June. - Sunday tutorials (IP1–IP9) have titles but no syllabi yet. Tutorial pages typically post 3–4 weeks pre-meeting. - Slide deposits. No central ISMB Zenodo community. Bioconductor speakers will deposit to zenodo.org/communities/bioconductor. Other slides ad-hoc; recheck individual lab sites post-meeting. - Recordings. ISCB Nucleus is the on-demand platform; recordings post incrementally over 2–4 weeks post-meeting and require login for full access.

    Verified high-level facts (2026-05-11)¶

    • Conference: ISMB 2026 — 34th Intelligent Systems for Molecular Biology
    • Dates: Sun Jul 12 – Thu Jul 16, 2026
    • Venue: Washington Hilton, Washington, DC, USA (hybrid; ISCB Nucleus virtual platform)
    • Theme: "A hub of science and global collaboration" — anniversary framing (>30 years of comp-bio)
    • Joint year? NO. This is ISMB-only. ISMB/ECCB joint year was 2025 (Liverpool). ECCB 2026 is a separate event: Geneva, Aug 31–Sep 4, 2026.
    • Scale: "Over 600 expected scientific talks" per ISCB framing; ~2000–3000 attendees historical baseline.
    • Proceedings: Online-only OUP Bioinformatics special issue. CC-BY. $700 author APC. Submission window Nov 11, 2025 – Jan 20, 2026 (closed). Notifications May 5, 2026. APC due May 7, 2026. Expected online publication ~mid-June 2026.
    • Abstracts: Window Jan 12 – Apr 9, 2026 (closed). Notifications May 5, 2026.
    • Distinguished Keynotes: 5 (one per day)
    • COSI tracks: 22 named COSIs + 7 special/closed tracks + 1 Open Session + 1 equity track + 1 publishing session + Industry Workshop + NIH/ODSS + Pathogen Data Network Forum + Q4LS (Quantum for Life Sciences) + WEB (Workshop on Education for Bioinformatics)
    • Tutorials: 9 Sunday Jul 12 full-day tutorials (IP1–IP9)
    • Nested events: BOSC 2026 (Jul 14–15) + Student Council Symposium (Sun) + DREAM Challenges (Sun) + CollaborationFest (Jul 17–18, post-conference)
